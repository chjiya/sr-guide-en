Cocolia Boss Guide

[Ad]

TL;DR

Cocolia summons Bronya as an ally who buffs her. Always kill Bronya first. Phase 2 features a charged AoE that must be interrupted by breaking Cocolia's toughness. Fire, Lightning, and Quantum DPS are most effective.

Community-Verified Strategy

Data from community boss guides shows the most common clear teams use Fire Trailblazer (taunt + Fire break) or any Fire/Lightning DPS. Ice DPS is ineffective due to Cocolia's 40% Ice RES.

Story Fight Tip

The Trailblazer (Fire) gets a special damage buff in the story version of this fight. Use them.

FAQ

What's the minimum level to beat Cocolia?

Level 30-35 with decent relics. The story fight scales down. Focus on breaking her toughness before the charged attack — the fight is a break check, not a DPS check.
