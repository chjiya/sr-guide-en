In the Night — Light Cone Guide

Overview

In the Night is Seele's signature Hunt path light cone. Its unique mechanic converts SPD into CRIT Rate and DMG% — the faster your character, the more damage they deal. This makes it a universally strong Hunt light cone.

Best Characters

  • Seele: Designed for her — her self-SPD buff maximizes the passive
  • Feixiao: Excellent alternative if you don't have her signature
  • Dr. Ratio: Benefits from the CRIT and DMG boost
  • Topaz: Good stat stick, though her signature is better for FUA teams
