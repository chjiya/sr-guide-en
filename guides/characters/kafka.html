Kafka Guide — DOT Enabler (v3.4)

Lightning · Nihility · S-Tier DOT Core
[Ad]

TL;DR

Kafka is the DOT team enabler. Her skill and ultimate instantly trigger all DOTs on enemies — Shock, Burn, Bleed, Wind Shear. Without Kafka, DOT teams tick once per enemy turn. With Kafka, they tick every time she acts (2-3x per cycle). Best team: Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo. Best relic: 4pc Prisoner in Deep Confinement.

Community Consensus

Kafka has remained S-tier since 1.2 — the longest meta relevance of any limited 5-star. In v3.4, DOT teams rank slightly behind Super Break and FUA in single-target but excel in multi-wave content (Pure Fiction). Community consensus: Kafka is the safest long-term investment due to DOT's universal applicability.

Relic & SPD

4pc Prisoner in Deep Confinement — 18% DEF ignore on DOT. Planar: Glamoth (135+ SPD) or IPC (if < 135 SPD). SPD target: 146+ is the community standard. Kafka needs to act before your other DOT appliers so she can trigger their DOTs on the same cycle.

FAQ

Is DOT still viable in 3.4?

Yes, but it's no longer #1. Super Break and FUA teams have surpassed DOT in single-target damage. DOT remains excellent in Pure Fiction and multi-wave content. Kafka + Black Swan still clears MoC 12 in 2-3 cycles.

Kafka without Black Swan?

Functional but significantly weaker. Sampo + Guinaifen is a budget alternative at approximately 60% of the premium team's damage. Only pull Kafka if you plan to eventually get Black Swan.
