Lightning · Nihility · Updated for Version 3.x

Why Kafka Defines the DOT Meta

Kafka is the undisputed queen of DOT (Damage Over Time) teams. Her skill and ultimate instantly trigger all DOT effects on enemies — including Shock, Burn, Bleed, and Wind Shear. This lets DOT teams front-load their damage instead of waiting for enemy turns.

How DOT Teams Work

DOT teams apply debuffs that deal damage when enemies take their turn. Kafka's gimmick is that she triggers these DOTs immediately without waiting. So your teammates stack DOTs (Black Swan's Arcana, Sampo's Wind Shear, Guinaifen's Burn), and Kafka detonates them every turn for massive damage.

Best Team Comps

Premium DOT

  • Kafka — DOT trigger
  • Black Swan — Arcana stacks (best DOT in game)
  • Ruan Mei / Robin — Team buffer
  • Huohuo — Healer + ATK buff

Budget DOT

  • Kafka — DOT trigger
  • Sampo — Wind Shear stacks
  • Guinaifen — Burn + Firekiss debuff
  • Any healer

Relic Recommendations

4-piece Prisoner in Deep Confinement (DOT set). Planar: Firmament Frontline: Glamoth. Main stats: ATK% body, SPD boots, Lightning DMG orb, ATK% rope. SPD is critical — aim for 146+.
