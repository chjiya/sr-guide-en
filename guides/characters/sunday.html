TL;DR

Sunday is a 5-star Imaginary Harmony character who specializes in action advance, energy battery support, and summon synergy. His kit is designed to accelerate a single ally's action while also empowering their summon (if applicable). He is the cornerstone of summon-based team compositions and also functions as a premium general support for any hypercarry team, especially those with high SP demands like DHIL or Jingliu.

Core Mechanics

Action Advance

Sunday's Skill advances a target ally's action by 100%, similar to Bronya. Unlike Bronya, Sunday also provides an energy battery effect and a CRIT DMG buff based on his own CDMG. His Skill costs 1 SP but the energy generation makes it worthwhile.

Summon Synergy

Sunday's kit uniquely advances both the target character AND their summon (such as Jing Yuan's Lightning Lord or Topaz's Numby). This makes him the definitive best support for summon-based DPS, as he effectively doubles the speed at which summon charges accumulate.

Stat Priority

  • SPD boots: SPD is king for action advancers
  • HP% / DEF% Body: Survivability
  • Energy Regen Rope: Mandatory for 3-turn ultimates
  • Substats: SPD > Effect RES > HP% > DEF%
  • Target: 134+ SPD (160+ ideal), enough ERR for 3-turn ult

Skill Priority

  1. Skill: Core ability — 100% action advance + energy + CRIT DMG
  2. Ultimate: Energy battery and team damage buff
  3. Talent: Passive CRIT DMG scaling
  4. Basic Attack: SP generator when not advancing

Best Team Compositions

Premium Summon: Sunday + Jing Yuan + Robin + Huohuo

The ultimate summon team. Sunday advances both Jing Yuan and Lightning Lord, Robin provides team-wide advance and ATK buffs, and Huohuo sustains while providing energy. This team maximizes Lightning Lord stacks for massive damage every cycle.

SP-Heavy: Sunday + DHIL + Tingyun + Luocha

Sunday enables DHIL's massive SP consumption by generating energy and providing efficient action control. Tingyun helps both Sunday and DHIL with energy, while Luocha provides SP-positive healing.

General Hypercarry: Sunday + Jingliu + Pela + Lynx

Sunday advances Jingliu through her enhanced state for maximum uptime. Pela provides DEF shred, and Lynx heals while also providing aggro manipulation for better survivability.

Eidolons & Resonance

  • E1: Skill DEF ignore on advanced ally — strong for hypercarry teams
  • E2: Energy on ult activation — smoother team rotations
  • E4: Team-wide SPD buff — excellent utility
  • E6: Skill becomes SP-positive — massive team flexibility

SPD Tuning Guide

SPD tuning is the most important aspect of building Sunday. The general rule: your DPS must be faster than Sunday by 1-5 SPD. This ensures the DPS acts first, then Sunday advances them for a second action in the same cycle.

  • 134 SPD setup: DPS at 134-135 SPD, Sunday at 133-134 SPD
  • 160+ SPD setup: DPS at 161+, Sunday at 160 (for extra cycle actions)
  • Hyperspeed Sunday: Sunday at 160+ with slow DPS — DPS acts twice per cycle

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sunday better than Bronya?

Sunday is generally stronger due to his energy battery, CRIT DMG buff on skill, and summon synergy. Bronya still has niches (wind DMG teams, higher base SPD for tuning). Pull Sunday if you have summon DPS; Bronya is still excellent as a second action advancer.

Does Sunday need his signature light cone?

No, but it's his best option. But the Battle Isn't Over (Bronya's cone) and Past and Future both work well. His signature's main advantage is SP efficiency over long fights.

How much Energy Regen does Sunday need?

With his signature, ERR rope gives a 3-turn ultimate. Without signature, you need ERR rope plus either an energy-supporting teammate (Huohuo, Tingyun) or his E2.

Can Sunday work without summon DPS?

Yes. He functions as a premium Bronya replacement in any hypercarry team. His energy battery and CRIT DMG buff are valuable regardless of summon presence. He's excellent with Jingliu, DHIL, Seele, and Blade.
