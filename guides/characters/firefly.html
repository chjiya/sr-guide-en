Firefly Guide — Super Break Core

Fire · Destruction · Updated for Version 3.x
[Ad Placeholder]

Why Firefly Dominates

Firefly is the centerpiece of the Super Break meta. In her enhanced state, she implants Fire weakness on enemies and deals massive Break damage. Unlike traditional DPS characters, Firefly scales with Break Effect instead of Crit — making her build surprisingly accessible.

How Super Break Works

When Firefly attacks a weakness-broken enemy, a portion of her damage is converted to Super Break damage. This scales with Break Effect and ignores enemy DEF. Combined with Harmony Trailblazer's ultimate (which enables Super Break for the whole team), Firefly shreds through bosses in record time.

Best Team Comps

Premium Super Break

  • Firefly — Main DPS
  • Harmony Trailblazer — Super Break enabler
  • Ruan Mei — Break efficiency + damage buff
  • Gallagher / Lingsha — Sustain + Break assist

Budget Option

  • Firefly — Main DPS
  • Harmony Trailblazer — Mandatory
  • Asta — SPD buff + Fire synergy
  • Gallagher — Healer

Relic Recommendations

4-piece Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge is her BiS. Planar: Forge of the Kalpagni Lantern. Main stats: ATK% body, SPD boots, ATK% orb, Break Effect rope. Aim for 150+ SPD and as much Break Effect as possible.

Light Cone Priority

Signature: "Whereabouts Should Dreams Rest". F2P: "On the Fall of an Aeon" (Herta shop) or "Under the Blue Sky".
