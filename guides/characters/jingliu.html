Ice · Destruction · Updated for Version 3.x

Why Jingliu Still Holds Up

Jingliu was the damage benchmark when she released in 1.4, and she's still a top-tier Ice DPS. Her gimmick is entering an enhanced "Spectral Transmigration" state where she gains massive CRIT Rate and drains a small amount of HP from allies to boost her damage. Simple to play, devastating when optimized.

How Her Kit Works

Use her skill to gain stacks of "Syzygy." Once you have enough stacks, she enters the Spectral Transmigration state. In this state, her skill becomes an enhanced AoE attack that consumes ally HP (a tiny amount) for massive damage. Her ultimate can be used in or out of enhanced state — it extends the state if she's already in it.

Best Team Comps

Hypercarry

  • Jingliu — Main DPS
  • Bronya — Action advance + DMG buff
  • Pela — AoE DEF shred (Ice synergy)
  • Huohuo / Luocha — Healer (offset HP drain)

Relic Recommendations

4-piece Hunter of Glacial Forest or 4-piece Genius of Brilliant Stars. Planar: Rutilant Arena. Main stats: Crit DMG body (she gets free CRIT Rate), SPD boots, Ice DMG orb, ATK% rope.
