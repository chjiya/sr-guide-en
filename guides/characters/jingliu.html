Jingliu Guide — Ice Hypercarry (v3.4)

Ice · Destruction · A-Tier DPS
[Ad]

TL;DR

Jingliu was the 1.4 damage benchmark and remains a solid Ice DPS in 3.4. She enters "Spectral Transmigration" state gaining 50% free CRIT Rate, then enhances her skill to an AoE blast. Best team: Jingliu + Bronya + Pela + Huohuo. Best relic: 4pc Hunter of Glacial Forest.

Community Consensus

Jingliu has fallen from SS-tier (1.4) to A-tier (3.4) due to HP inflation and the rise of Super Break/FUA. She still clears MoC 12 in 4-5 cycles at E0S1 investment — functional but no longer top-tier. Community recommendation: only pull if you specifically need Ice coverage or like her playstyle.

FAQ

Is Jingliu still worth pulling in 3.4?

Only if you lack Ice DPS and like her. For meta value, Acheron, Firefly, and Feixiao are better investments. Jingliu clears content but requires more investment than newer DPS.

Jingliu or Yanqing?

Jingliu, vastly. She has higher base stats, built-in CRIT Rate, and AoE coverage. Yanqing requires Gepard to function and still underperforms.
