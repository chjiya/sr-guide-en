TL;DR

Boothill is a 5-star Physical Hunt character who specializes in single-target Break damage. His Standoff mechanic lets him duel a single enemy, stacking Trickshot for massive Break-enhanced attacks. He is one of the best single-target DPS in the game for Physical-weak content and a core member of the Super Break meta alongside Harmony Trailblazer and Ruan Mei.

Core Mechanics

Standoff

Boothill's Skill initiates a Standoff with one enemy, locking him into a duel. During Standoff, he gains Taunt and stacks Trickshot whenever he or the enemy takes action. At max stacks, his next attack consumes them for massive Break damage that ignores a portion of enemy Toughness.

Trickshot Stacks

Each time Boothill is hit or lands a Basic Attack during Standoff, he gains a Trickshot stack (max 3). His enhanced Basic Attack at 3 stacks deals massive Toughness Reduction and Break damage. Managing Standoff duration and stack generation is key to his gameplay.

Best Team Compositions

Premium Super Break: Boothill + HMC + Ruan Mei + Gallagher

This is Boothill's strongest team. Harmony Trailblazer enables Super Break damage on Broken enemies, Ruan Mei provides team-wide Weakness Break Efficiency and RES PEN, and Gallagher offers SP-positive healing with Break synergy. This team clears any Physical-weak MoC floor with ease.

Budget: Boothill + Pela + Bronya + Lynx

Pela provides DEF shred for higher damage, Bronya advances Boothill for faster Trickshot stacking, and Lynx keeps the team alive. This team is accessible but lacks the Super Break damage of the premium version.

Hypercarry: Boothill + Bronya + Tingyun + Huohuo

Focuses on raw Boothill damage through multiple actions. Bronya and Tingyun both advance/boost Boothill, while Huohuo provides energy and healing. Works well against non-Physical-weak enemies where Break isn't guaranteed.

Eidolons & Resonance

  • E1: Reduces enemy Toughness even when off-element — excellent value
  • E2: Energy on Standoff end — smoother rotation
  • E4: Team Break Effect buff — good support value
  • E6: Massive damage spike — Standoff enhanced attacks can multi-target

Rotation Tips

Standard rotation: Skill (initiate Standoff) → Basic Attack × 1-2 (build Trickshot) → Enhanced Basic Attack at 3 stacks → Reapply Standoff. In Super Break teams, always ensure Ruan Mei's field and HMC's ult are active before using Boothill's enhanced attack for maximum Break damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Boothill better than Firefly?

Both are top-tier Break DPS. Firefly is better in AoE with self-implanting Fire weakness. Boothill is better against single-target Physical-weak bosses. They want different teams, so pulling both is fine if you have the resources.

Does Boothill need CRIT stats?

No. Boothill's damage primarily comes from Break effects, which don't crit. Focus entirely on Break Effect and SPD. CRIT stats are wasted on him.

What SPD breakpoint should I aim for?

145 SPD is the minimum to activate Talia's 4-piece bonus. 160+ SPD is ideal for more actions in early cycles, especially with Bronya support.

Is Boothill's light cone necessary?

No. Cruising in the Stellar Sea (free from Herta shop) and Swordplay both perform well. His signature is roughly 15-20% better, but not mandatory for clearing content.

Can Boothill break off-element enemies?

With E1, yes — though much slower. Without E1, he struggles against non-Physical-weak enemies. His enhanced attack still deals good damage, but Break damage is significantly reduced.
