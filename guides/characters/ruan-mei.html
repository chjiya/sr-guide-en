Ruan Mei Guide — Universal Break Support

Ice · Harmony · Updated for Version 3.x
[Ad Placeholder]

Why Ruan Mei Is in Every Team

Ruan Mei is the most versatile support in Honkai: Star Rail. She provides team-wide DMG%, RES PEN, SPD, and Break Efficiency — every stat a DPS wants. Whether you're running Hypercarry, DOT, or Break teams, Ruan Mei fits perfectly.

Break Efficiency Explained

Ruan Mei's unique contribution is Break Efficiency — it makes attacks deal more Toughness damage to enemy shields. This means faster breaks, more damage windows, and more turns where enemies can't act. In Super Break teams with Harmony Trailblazer, this is multiplicative.

Best Team Comps

Ruan Mei works in literally every team. Her best pairings:

  • Super Break: Firefly + Harmony TB + Ruan Mei
  • DOT: Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei
  • Hypercarry: Any DPS + Bronya + Ruan Mei

Relic Recommendations

4-piece Watchmaker, Master of Dream Machinations (Break team) or 4-piece Messenger Traversing Hackerspace (general). Planar: Broken Keel. Aim for 160+ SPD and as much Break Effect as you can stack.
