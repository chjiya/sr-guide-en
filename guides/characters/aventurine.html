Aventurine Guide — Community Consensus Build (v3.4)

Imaginary · Preservation · T0 Sustain

TL;DR — Quick Answer

Aventurine is the best Preservation sustain in Honkai: Star Rail as of version 3.4, with unparalleled synergy in follow-up attack (FUA) teams. His kit revolves around applying and maintaining a party-wide Fortified Shield that stacks with each FUA he or his allies perform. His talent provides a follow-up attack that both reapplies shields and deals respectable Imaginary damage. He also grants the entire team 50% Effect RES, making him the premier debuff-resistant sustain. Best team: Feixiao + Robin + Topaz + Aventurine. Best relic: 4pc Knight of Purity Palace or 4pc Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge.

Community Consensus: How Good Is Aventurine?

Aventurine is rated T0 (SS-tier) by Prydwen, NGA, and Reddit community tier lists since his release in version 2.1. In MoC 3.4 data, Aventurine appears on over 55% of FUA teams clearing Floor 12. He is considered an essential pull for FUA teams and a strong general-purpose sustain for any team where allies trigger multiple attacks per turn. Community consensus places him as the best overall sustain alongside Fu Xuan and Huohuo, but uniquely flexible due to his debuff application for Acheron teams and his FUA synergy.

How His Kit Works (Community-Verified Mechanics)

Aventurine's defensive and offensive utility is tied to his follow-up attack loop:

  • Skill: Applies the Fortified Shield to the entire party for 3 turns. The shield value scales with DEF. The shield can stack up to 200% of its base value when reapplied through FUA procs.
  • Talent (FUA): When any ally performs a follow-up attack, Aventurine gains 1 stack of his talent (Blind Bet). At 7 stacks, he automatically uses his follow-up attack against a random enemy — dealing Imaginary damage and reapplying a portion of his shield to the whole party. Each FUA also has a chance to trigger the talent's additional damage effect.
  • Ultimate: Deals AoE Imaginary damage and applies a debuff that increases enemy CRIT DMG taken by 9-15% (depending on talent level). This debuff counts for Acheron's Slashed Dream stack generation.
  • Team Effect RES: Aventurine's trace passively grants the entire team 50% Effect RES while his Fortified Shield is active. This is a massive defensive boost that massively reduces the impact of enemy CC and debuff mechanics.

Key community-verified interaction: Aventurine's shield stacks persist through turns. If you skill then trigger 2 FUAs before his next turn, the party effectively has 3x shield strength. This means Aventurine can skip skill casts for multiple turns in high-FUA teams, generating SP for the rest of the party.

General Sustain (Any Team)

Even outside FUA teams, Aventurine provides one of the strongest shields in the game. His 50% team-wide Effect RES makes him the premier choice against CC-heavy enemies like Kafka (Dominance) or the Swarm. His only weakness is against DoT-heavy content where raw healing outstrips shield-only sustain.

Relic & Light Cone Priority (Tested Rankings)

Relic Sets

  1. 4pc Knight of Purity Palace — Best for maximum shield strength. 15% DEF + 20% shield strength bonus. Your shields will be ~25% stronger than any alternative.
  2. 4pc Iron Cavalry Against the Scourge — Alternative for hybrid damage/shield. Good if you want Aventurine to contribute meaningful damage alongside shielding. The 25%/50% DEF ignore on Toughness-broken enemies adds decent damage.
  3. 2pc Knight + 2pc Guard of Wuthering Snow — Budget option. 15% DEF + 12% DEF from Guard set. Pure DEF stacking.

Planar Ornaments

  1. Broken Keel — Best support option. 10% CRIT DMG for team + 10% Effect RES (helps Aventurine reach 50% Effect RES for his trace).

  3. Penacony, Land of the Dreams — Niche option. 5% ERR (helps ultimate debuff uptime) + 10% DMG bonus for same-element allies (Imaginary DPS only).

Frequently Asked Questions

DEF vs Effect RES — which stat do I prioritize?

Prioritize DEF until you reach 4000 DEF, then invest in Effect RES. 4000 DEF is the community-verified breakpoint where Aventurine's shield becomes comfortable for MoC 12 content. After that, Effect RES substats become valuable because (a) Aventurine's trace gives 50% team-wide Effect RES while shielded, (b) Broken Keel requires 30% Effect RES for the team CRIT DMG buff, and (c) preventing Aventurine from being CC'd ensures his FUA keeps running. Ideally reach 30-50% personal Effect RES.

Is Aventurine's shield 100% uptime possible?

Yes, easily in FUA teams; requires skill management otherwise. Aventurine's skill applies a 3-turn shield. In FUA teams, his talent FUA procs reapply shield stacks automatically, so the shield never expires. In non-FUA teams, you need to skill every 3 turns. With 134+ SPD, his rotation is: Skill (turn 1) → basic ×2 (turns 2-3) → Skill (turn 4). Shield stacks can reach 200% of base value with frequent FUA triggers.

What SPD does Aventurine need?

134 SPD is the minimum recommendation; 143+ is comfortable. At 134 SPD, Aventurine gets 2 actions in the first MoC cycle, allowing a quick skill → basic → skill rotation. At 143+ SPD, he cycles slightly faster, which improves ultimate uptime and the debuff application for Acheron/Dr. Ratio teams. However, DEF% boots are viable if you have high SPD substats — the community consensus is SPD > DEF% on boots only if you can hit at least 134 SPD with substats.

Is Aventurine worth pulling in 3.4?

Yes, especially if you run FUA or Acheron teams. Aventurine is the best sustain for FUA compositions and one of the best for Acheron teams due to his ultimate debuff. If you have Feixiao, Topaz, Ratio, or Acheron, Aventurine is a top priority pull. Even as a general sustain, he provides stronger shields and better Effect RES than any alternative. The only skip case is if you already have Fu Xuan + Huohuo and don't play FUA teams.
