Feixiao Guide — Follow-Up Attack Queen

Wind · Hunt · Updated for Version 3.x
[Ad Placeholder]

Why Feixiao Is Meta

Feixiao is the premier single-target DPS in the current meta. Her gimmick is follow-up attacks — she launches extra attacks whenever allies hit enemies, stacking up massive damage in a single turn cycle. She's the core of the FUA (Follow-Up Attack) archetype.

How Her Kit Works

Feixiao's skill does solid damage and gives her a buff. Her talent accumulates "Flying Aureus" stacks whenever any ally attacks an enemy (up to a cap per turn). At full stacks, she unleashes a powerful follow-up attack. Her ultimate consumes these stacks for a devastating single-target nuke.

Best Team Comps

Premium FUA Team

  • Feixiao — Main DPS
  • Topaz — Follow-up damage amplifier (+50% FUA damage on marked target)
  • Robin — Team-wide ATK buff, extra damage on every attack
  • Aventurine — Sustain with follow-up attacks

Budget Option

  • Feixiao — Main DPS
  • March 7th (Hunt) — Free FUA support
  • Tingyun — ATK + energy battery
  • Lynx — Healer

Relic Recommendations

4-piece The Wind-Soaring Valorous is her BiS. Planar: Duran, Dynasty of Running Wolves. Main stats: Crit Rate body, SPD boots, Wind DMG orb, ATK% rope.

Light Cone Priority

Signature: "I Venture Forth to Hunt". F2P: "Cruising in the Stellar Sea" (Herta shop) or "Swordplay".
