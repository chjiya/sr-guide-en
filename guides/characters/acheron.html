TL;DR — Quick Answer

Acheron is the strongest Lightning DPS in Honkai: Star Rail as of version 3.4. Her ultimate ignores weakness types and charges from team-wide debuff application rather than energy. Best team: Acheron + Jiaoqiu + Pela + Aventurine. Best relic: 4pc Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters. Her signature light cone "Along the Passing Shore" provides a ~25% damage increase over the best F2P option.

Community Consensus: How Good Is Acheron?

Based on aggregated data from community tier lists (Prydwen, Reddit r/HonkaiStarRail polls, NGA), Acheron has been consistently ranked SS-tier since her release in 2.1. In version 3.4 Memory of Chaos data tracked by community spreadsheet compilers, Acheron teams achieve an average cycle clear of 2.1 on Floor 12 — placing her in the top 3 DPS characters alongside Firefly and Feixiao.

How Her Kit Works (Community-Verified Mechanics)

Acheron's skill applies a debuff that increases damage taken by 15% for 2 turns. Her talent accumulates "Slashed Dream" stacks when ANY debuff is applied by any ally — this includes debuffs from light cones (e.g., Pearl LC on Pela), relic effects, and character abilities. At 9 stacks, her ultimate becomes available: a 3-hit AoE Lightning attack that reduces enemy Toughness regardless of weakness type and deals 30% increased damage to debuffed targets.

Key mechanic verified by community testing: Acheron's ultimate damage snapshot is taken at the moment of cast, not per hit. This means buffs applied before the first slash apply to all three slashes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Acheron worth pulling in 3.4?

Yes, if you have Jiaoqiu or plan to get him. Acheron without Jiaoqiu is approximately 30% weaker. With Jiaoqiu, she's still a top-3 DPS. If you don't have and won't get Jiaoqiu, consider pulling Firefly or Feixiao instead.

Does Acheron need her E2?

No. E0S1 is sufficient for all content. E2 lets you run only one Nihility teammate instead of two (freeing a slot for Harmony), which is a ~15% team damage increase. E0 with Jiaoqiu + Pela already clears MoC 12 comfortably.

E0S1 or E2S0?

E0S1 is better. Her signature light cone provides a larger damage increase (25%) than E1 (10%). Community consensus: pull priority is S1 > Jiaoqiu > E2.

Can I use Acheron without Jiaoqiu?

Yes, but expect 2-3 extra cycles in MoC. Pela + Guinaifen is a functional budget alternative. Your clear speed will be slower, but you'll still clear. For Pure Fiction, Acheron is strong even without Jiaoqiu.

What speed should Acheron have?

134 SPD minimum, 143+ recommended. Acheron needs speed to apply her skill debuff and generate energy (stacks). ATK boots are only viable if you run her with Sparkle or Bronya for action advance.
