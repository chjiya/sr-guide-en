Acheron Guide — The Ultimate T0 DPS

Lightning · Nihility · Updated for Version 3.x

Why Acheron Is Top Tier

Acheron is the undisputed queen of damage in Honkai: Star Rail. Her ultimate bypasses the usual energy system — she charges it by applying debuffs to enemies. This lets her spam her devastating ultimate far more often than any other DPS character.

How Her Kit Works

Acheron's skill applies a debuff that increases damage taken. Her talent stacks charges whenever any ally applies a debuff — including debuffs from light cones and relics. Once she hits enough stacks, her ultimate becomes available: a massive AoE Lightning attack that ignores weakness types.

The key to playing Acheron well is team composition. You need teammates who can apply debuffs consistently. Pela (AoE DEF shred), Silver Wolf (single-target debuffs), and Jiaoqiu (ultimate vulnerability debuff) are her best partners.

Best Team Comps

Premium Team

  • Acheron — Main DPS
  • Jiaoqiu — Ultimate vulnerability, debuff spam
  • Pela — AoE DEF shred
  • Fu Xuan / Aventurine — Sustain

Budget Team

  • Acheron — Main DPS
  • Pela — AoE DEF shred
  • Guinaifen — Burn debuff
  • Gallagher — Sustain with debuff on ult

Relic Recommendations

4-piece Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters is her best set. For planar ornaments, Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm is tailored for her. Prioritize Crit Rate/DMG body, ATK% boots, Lightning DMG orb, and ATK% rope.

Light Cone Priority

Her signature light cone "Along the Passing Shore" is the best. F2P options: "Good Night and Sleep Well" (4-star, great debuff synergy) or "Fermata" (4-star, accessible).

FAQ

Is Acheron worth pulling?

Absolutely. She's the best Lightning DPS and one of the strongest characters in the game. Even at E0, she clears all endgame content comfortably.

Does Acheron need her signature light cone?

It helps a lot, but she's perfectly functional with 4-star alternatives. Prioritize getting Jiaoqiu first if you have to choose.
