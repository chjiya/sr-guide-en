📋 MoC Overview

Memory of Chaos (MoC) is Honkai: Star Rail's primary endgame mode, resetting every 2 weeks. It consists of 12 floors (Phase 1 originally, plus additional stages added over time), with each floor split into two halves. Your goal is to clear both halves within a limited number of cycles (Action Value system) to earn stars. 3-starring each floor is required for maximum rewards.

Reward Structure: Stellar Jade rewards scale with star count. Maximum rewards (all 12 floors × 3 stars = 36 stars total) grant 600-800 Stellar Jade per reset, varying slightly by version. This is approximately 750 Stellar Jade per reset on average, or roughly 1,500 per month — equivalent to about 9-10 limited banner pulls per month from MoC alone. Combined with Pure Fiction and Apocalyptic Shadow, the three endgame modes give approximately 2,200-2,400 Stellar Jade per month.

Archetype Distribution: The ideal MoC clear uses two different archetypes for the two halves. For example, Break on one half and FUA on the other. This avoids gear competition (you can use Robin on one half and Ruan Mei on the other) and ensures you're immune to single-target debuffs (a boss that specifically counters one archetype won't ruin both halves).

👥 Team Building for Both Halves

Building two teams for MoC requires managing resources across 8 characters (or more if you need replacements). Here's how to approach it:

  • Sustain Allocation: Your stronger sustain (Aventurine, Fu Xuan, Huohuo, Lingsha) should go to the harder half. The easier half can use Gallagher, Bailu, Lynx, or Natasha. Generally, the second half is harder than the first half in current MoC rotations.
  • Harmony/Nihility Allocation: Ruan Mei, Robin, Sparkle, and Jiaoqiu are in high demand. Pre-plan which half gets which limited support. Ruan Mei goes best with Break, Robin with FUA, Sparkle with DHIL/Jingliu, Jiaoqiu with Acheron.
  • Element Coverage: Ensure your two teams cover different weaknesses. If both halves feature Ice-weak enemies, you'll need to split your Ice DPS options wisely. Use the in-game enemy preview before starting.
  • Gear Sharing: You cannot use the same relic set or light cone on two characters in different halves. Plan your gear distribution accordingly. The best strategy is to have two dedicated DPS sets with their own supports' gear pre-configured.

🎯 Current Rotation Tips (v3.4)

For the current v3.4 MoC rotation, here are the key considerations:

  • Typically features weaker enemies or a boss that's easier to handle. The HP thresholds are lower in the first half

  • The harder half with a main boss featuring high HP and potentially dangerous mechanics
  • Recommended: Break (Firefly) against bosses with high Toughness bars, or FUA (Feixiao) for single-target boss burns
  • Boss mechanics to watch: enemies that apply debuffs (bring cleanse — Huohuo, Lynx, or effect RES), enemies with self-buffs (use Pela or Luocha to dispel), enemies that summon (bring AoE or they'll overwhelm you)

💡 3-Star Tips & Tricks

  • Reset for RNG: If your first attempt is going poorly (bad RNG on enemy targeting, missed crits), don't be afraid to restart. The cycle system means one bad enemy attack that stuns your DPS can cost you 2+ cycles.
  • Know When to Ultimate: Wasting Ultimate energy by using it at 90% enemy HP is inefficient. Save ultimates for when they can overkill enemies or break elite enemy Toughness bars at critical moments.
  • Stratified Clear: You don't need to 3-star a floor in one run. You can clear first half with a slow run (0 stars) and second half quickly (3 stars), then re-clear first half quickly. Individual half star counts are tracked separately. Many players first ensure they can survive both halves, then optimize for speed.
  • Turn Manipulation: Certain characters can manipulate turn order to squeeze in extra actions. Bronya and Sparkle are the most obvious, but also consider using Dance Dance Dance light cone (Herta shop) on supports for Action Advance on Ultimate.
  • Don't Ignore Blessings: The simulated blessing mechanics within MoC (pick between injury/memory/abundance blessings between stages) can have significant impact. Always check what the next blessing options grant before making your choice.

❓ FAQ

When does MoC reset?

MoC resets every 2 weeks, on Monday at 04:00 server time. The monthly Stellar Jade income from MoC is roughly 1,500 per month (2 resets × ~750 per reset).

What's the minimum investment to clear MoC?

To 3-star all 12 floors, you need two teams of level 80 characters with level 8+ traces and +12-15 relics. A typical "first clear" account has 2 limited S-rank DPS characters with their best 4-star supports. Full F2P clears are possible with well-invested 4-star characters (Herta, Sushang, Xueyi, Qingque) as main DPS plus standard 5-star supports (Bronya, Pela, Tingyun, Asta). Expect to need 20-30 cycles total for a comfortable 36-star clear.

Should I pull for eidolons or new characters for MoC?

New characters > eidolons, in general. Having two competent teams (each with a limited 5-star DPS and standard/4-star supports) is the most important milestone. Once you have 4+ limited 5-star characters forming two strong teams, MoC becomes significantly easier. Eidolons are for vertical investment after your horizontal roster is already strong. The best MoC eidolons to target: Ruan Mei E1 (team DEF ignore), Robin E1 (team RES pen), Acheron E2 (team building flexibility, lets her run without another Nihility).
