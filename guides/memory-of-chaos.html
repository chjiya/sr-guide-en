📋 What Is Memory of Chaos?

Memory of Chaos (MoC) is Honkai: Star Rail's flagship endgame mode, resetting twice per month (1st and 16th). It consists of 12 stages across two halves. To earn all stars, you must clear each floor within 20 total cycles across both halves. The difficulty ramps sharply at floors 10-12, where enemy HP pools reach 1.5M+ per wave. A full clear nets ~720 Stellar Jades monthly from the high floors alone — essential income for any account.

💰 MoC Reward Breakdown (Floors 10-12)

  • MoC 10 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + Refined Aether ×3 + Lost Crystal ×3
  • MoC 11 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + Lost Crystal ×4 + Eternal Endurance ×2
  • MoC 12 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + Eternal Endurance ×4
  • Total Monthly: ~720 Stellar Jades + large material rewards

⚡ Current v3.4 Turbulence — "Crystalline Storm"

The v3.4 Memory Turbulence Crystalline Storm is a follow-up attack (FUA) enabler. Every time an ally lands a FUA against a Weakness-Broken enemy, it triggers bonus 50% True Damage (ignores DEF and RES) and regenerates 15 Energy for the attacking ally. This turbulence heavily favors FUA teams and Break compositions that can reliably keep enemies broken.

🥈 A-Tier: DoT

DoT is consistent and bypasses RES (Black Swan's stacking), making it excellent against high-RES bosses. However, DoT doesn't trigger FUA, so you miss the Crystalline Storm bonus. Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo remains strong but requires specific relic builds.

🥈 A-Tier: Hypercarry

Acheron, Jingliu, and DHIL can still clear MoC 12, but they struggle with the multi-target waves where FUA excels. Acheron with Jiaoqiu is the strongest hypercarry option. Jingliu needs Bronya to keep up with cycle counts.

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor — MoC 10

First Half — Aurumaton + Automatons

Enemies: Wave 1: Automaton Spiders ×2, Automaton Hound. Wave 2: Aurumaton Spectral Envoy + Automaton Direwolves ×2.

The Aurumaton resists Physical, Fire, and Lightning but is weak to Quantum, Wind, and Imaginary. Use Feixiao (Wind) or Dr. Ratio (Imaginary) for type advantage. A Seele mono-Quantum team also shreds this half.

Recommended: Feixiao + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine (1-2 cycles). Seele + Silver Wolf + Sparkle + Fuxuan (2-3 cycles).

Second Half — Stellaron Hunter: Kafka

Enemies: Wave 1: Voidranger Reavers ×2 + Mara-Struck Soldier. Wave 2: Kafka.

Kafka applies DoT to your team. Bring a healer with cleanse — Huohuo or Luocha are best. Natasha works as a free option. Firefly Break destroys this half since Kafka is weak to Physical, Lightning, and Fire.

Recommended: Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Gallagher (1-2 cycles). Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo (2-3 cycles).

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor — MoC 11

First Half — Gepard + Silvermane Guards

Enemies: Wave 1: Incineration Shadewalkers + Gunners. Wave 2: Gepard with his impenetrable shield.

Gepard's shield requires burst damage to break before he reapplies it. He's weak to Lightning and Quantum but resistant to Ice. Do not bring Jingliu here. Acherone or Kafka work well.

Recommended: Acheron + Jiaoqiu + Pela + Fuxuan (2-3 cycles). Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo (2-3 cycles).

Second Half — Yanqing + Frost Guards

Enemies: Wave 1: Frost Guards. Wave 2: Yanqing + more Frost Guards.

Yanqing's Soulsteel Sync makes him dodge single-target attacks. Kill his guards first with AoE damage, then focus him. Yanqing is weak to Fire, Physical, and Imaginary.

Recommended: Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Lingsha (2 cycles). Dr. Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine (2-3 cycles).

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor — MoC 12

⭐ Best MoC 12 Clear Teams

First Half: Feixiao / Topaz / Robin / Aventurine — Numby and Aventurine FUA procs fuel Crystalline Storm for 300K+ bonus damage per wave.

Second Half: Firefly / HMC / Ruan Mei / Lingsha — Lingsha's FUA triggers the turbulence, adding free True Damage to the Break rotation.

First Half — True Sting + Swarm Hive

Wave 1: Lesser Stings ×3 (they multiply if not killed quickly). Wave 2: True Sting boss.

The True Sting spawns adds that charge its ultimate. AoE is critical — kill adds the moment they appear. The boss is weak to Wind, Lightning, and Imaginary. Feixiao (Wind) or Dr. Ratio (Imaginary) are your best bets.

Best Teams:

  • Feixiao + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine — 1-2 cycle clear (S-tier)
  • Dr. Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine — consistent 2-3 cycles
  • Acheron + Jiaoqiu + Pela + Fuxuan — 3-4 cycles

Second Half — Cocolia + Bronya Duo

Wave 1: IPC Grunts ×2 + Sentinel. Wave 2: Cocolia + Bronya together.

Bronya action-advances Cocolia, enabling devastating combos. Priority: Break Cocolia first to stop her AoE freeze. Cocolia is weak to Fire, Lightning, Physical. Bronya is weak to Ice, Imaginary, Physical. Firefly and Boothill excel here.

Best Teams:

  • Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Lingsha — 2-3 cycle clear
  • Boothill + Ruan Mei + Bronya + Gallagher — 2-3 cycles, burst the duo down one at a time
  • Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo — 3-4 cycles

Cycle Efficiency Tips

  1. Pre-charge All Ults: Enter MoC 12 with full energy. Burst the first wave in 0 cycles for maximum time savings.
  2. Weakness Match: Don't force a team that matches fewer than 2/3 of enemy weaknesses. The Toughness damage penalty costs 1-2 full cycles.
  3. Skip Ult Animations: Press ESC during Ult animations to skip them. This can save 3-5 seconds per ultimate — enough for an extra cycle late in the fight.
  4. Know When to Reset: If your first half takes 5+ cycles, reset. MoC 12 demands ~4 cycles per half for full stars.
  5. AoE on Multi-adds: True Sting and Yanqing demand AoE. Use Herta or Argenti as a second DPS if your main team lacks it.

🔄 Advanced Rotation & Energy Management

Energy management is the difference between a 3-cycle and 5-cycle clear. Stack these sources:

  • Energy Rope (ERR): Supports need ERR ropes. Robin needs ~120% ERR for a 3-turn Ultimate.
  • Huohuo Ult: +20% Energy to all allies — shaves one full cycle off your clear.
  • Planar Sets: Vonwacq (5% ERR + turn 1 SPD) and Penacony/Lushaka (5% ERR + ATK%) are excellent support options.
  • Ult Timing: Never press a support Ult unless your DPS's turn is next. Wasting buff uptime on a healer/sustainer turn costs cycles.
