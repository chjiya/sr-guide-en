📋 Memory of Chaos Overview

Memory of Chaos (MoC) is Honkai: Star Rail's primary endgame mode, resetting twice per month (1st and 16th). It consists of 12 stages across two halves, where players must clear each floor within 20 cycles for full stars. The reward structure scales with clear speed: 3★ per floor requires 20 cycles or fewer total across both halves, with the final stages — MoC 10, 11, and 12 — awarding the most Stellar Jades and valuable upgrade materials.

💰 MoC Reward Summary

  • MoC 10 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + 60 Trailblaze EXP + Refined Aether ×3 + Lost Crystal ×3
  • MoC 11 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + 60 Trailblaze EXP + Lost Crystal ×4 + Eternal Endurance ×2
  • MoC 12 (3★): 120 Stellar Jades + 60 Trailblaze EXP + Eternal Endurance ×4
  • Total Monthly: ~720 Stellar Jades from MoC 10-12 alone

⚡ Current v3.4 Turbulence — "Crystalline Storm"

The v3.4 Memory Turbulence, Crystalline Storm, triggers a secondary effect every time an ally uses a follow-up attack that hits an enemy Weakness Broken target. When triggered, it deals 50% True Damage (ignores DEF and RES) split across all enemies, plus regenerates 15 Energy for the attacking ally. This heavily favors Follow-Up Attack (FUA) teams and Break compositions that can consistently trigger Weakness Break on demand.

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor Strategy — MoC 10

First Half — Aurumaton & Automaton Squad

Wave 1 features two Automaton Spiders and an Automaton Hound. Wave 2 spawns an Aurumaton Spectral Envoy backed by two Automaton Direwolfs. The Aurumaton has high Physical, Fire, and Lightning RES but is weak to Quantum, Wind, and Imaginary. Bring a Quantum or Wind DPS to shred its Toughness bar efficiently.

Recommended: Feixiao + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine (FUA core). Seele + Silver Wolf + Bronya + Fuxuan (mono-Quantum hypercarry) also works well.

Second Half — Stellaron Hunter: Kafka

The first wave is two Voidranger: Reavers and a Mara-Struck Soldier. Wave 2 is Kafka herself. She applies constant DoT which punishes slow-clearing teams. Bring a healer with cleanse (Huohuo, Luocha, or Natasha) and a debuff removal support. Kafka is weak to Physical, Lightning, and Fire — making a Firefly or Kafka DoT team ideal for this side.

Recommended: Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Gallagher (Break). Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo (DoT).

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor Strategy — MoC 11

First Half — Gepard & Silvermane Guards

Wave 1 contains Incineration Shadewalkers and Silvermane Gunners. Wave 2 is Gepard with his shield. You need high burst damage to break his shield before he re-applies it. Gepard is weak to Lightning and Quantum while resistant to Ice. Jingliu teams struggle here — favor Acheron, Kafka, or Seele instead.

Recommended: Acheron + Pela + Silver Wolf + Fuxuan (Hypercarry). Seele + Sparkle + Bronya + Huohuo (mono-Quantum).

Second Half — Yanqing & Frost Guard

Yanqing arrives in Wave 2 with Frost Guards protecting him. His Soulsteel Sync mechanic makes him dodge attacks unless you break his teammates first. AoE damage is key to clearing the guards quickly. Yanqing is weak to Fire, Physical, and Imaginary.

Recommended: Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Lingsha (Break). Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine (FUA).

🏗️ Floor-by-Floor Strategy — MoC 12

⭐ MoC 12 Best Clear Teams

First Half: Feixiao / Topaz / Robin / Aventurine — the Crystalline Storm turbulence gives this team infinite value. Every Numby and Aventurine follow-up procs True Damage and erodes the boss bar faster than any other comp.

Second Half: Firefly / HMC / Ruan Mei / Lingsha — high Breaking speed keeps the turbulence active. Lingsha's follow-ups also trigger the turbulence, adding extra True Damage per rotation.

First Half — True Sting & Swarm Hive

Wave 1 spawns Lesser Stings that multiply if not cleared quickly. Wave 2 is the True Sting boss with its AoE swarm attacks. The True Sting is weak to Wind, Lightning, and Imaginary — a FUA team with Feixiao (Wind) or Ratio (Imaginary) excels here. The boss spawns adds that charge its ultimate; kill them promptly.

Best Teams:

  • Feixiao + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine — ~1-2 cycle clear
  • Dr. Ratio + Topaz + Robin + Aventurine — consistent 2-3 cycles
  • Acheron + Jiaoqiu + Pela + Fuxuan — 3-4 cycles if properly built

Second Half — Cocolia & Bronya Duo

The most demanding MoC 12 half. Wave 1 has two IPC Grunts and a Sentinel. Wave 2 pits you against Cocolia and Bronya together — Bronya will action-advance Cocolia for devastating combos. Cocolia is weak to Fire, Lightning, and Physical; Bronya is weak to Ice, Imaginary, and Physical. Break the correct enemy to control the fight — prioritize breaking Cocolia first to stop her AoE freeze.

Best Teams:

  • Firefly + HMC + Ruan Mei + Gallagher — 2-3 cycle clear
  • Boothill + Ruan Mei + Bronya + Lingsha — 2-3 cycles, single-target focus on Bronya first
  • Kafka + Black Swan + Ruan Mei + Huohuo — 3-4 cycles, DoT works through both toughness bars
  • Jingliu + Bronya + Ruan Mei + Huohuo — 4-5 cycles, second-best Ice option

⏱️ Cycle Management Tips

Cycle Efficiency Checklist:

  1. Pre-charge Energy: Enter MoC 12 with all Ults ready — 0-cycle the first wave for maximum time savings.
  2. Weakness Match: Never force a team that doesn't match at least 2/3 enemy weaknesses. The Toughness damage penalty is too severe.
  3. Speed Tuning: At 134 SPD, your DPS gets 2 actions in cycle 1. At 160+ SPD with action advance (Bronya, Sparkle), you can get 3-4 actions before the cycle counter increments.
  4. AoE vs ST: On multi-add waves (True Sting, Yanqing guards), use AoE characters. On single-boss floors (Kafka, Gepard), single-target burst shines.
  5. Reset Wisely: If your first half takes more than 5 cycles, reset and adjust your strategy. MoC 12 requires ~4 cycles per half for full stars.
  6. Ult Animation Skip: Press ESC or click during Ult animations to skip them and save precious seconds (especially for 0-cycle runs).

🔄 Advanced Rotation & Energy Management

Energy management separates a 3-cycle clear from a 5-cycle clear in MoC 12. The Crystalline Storm turbulence already gives +15 Energy per FUA trigger, but you can stack additional energy sources:

  • Energy Rope (ERR): Almost all supports (Robin, Ruan Mei, Bronya, Tingyun) want an Energy Recharge Rope. Robin needs ~120% ERR to 3-turn Ultimate without hit RNG.
  • Huohuo's Ultimate: Grants 20% Energy to all allies. This can shave a full cycle off your clear time by letting your DPS get their Ultimate one rotation earlier.
  • Energy Planar Sets: Vonwacq (1 turn SPD boost + 5% ERR) and Penacony (10% ERR for same-type allies) are excellent for supports who need tight rotations.
  • Ult Timing: Never press a support Ult unless your DPS's turn is coming up next. Wasting buff uptime on a healer turn costs cycles.

🍃 Rotation Reference — Feixiao FUA Team:

  • Turn 1: Robin Skill → Feixiao Skill → Aventurine FUA → Numby FUA → Robin Ult (3-turn rotation with ERR rope)
  • Turn 2: Feixiao Ult → Feixiao Skill → Numby + Aventurine FUA chains → Feixiao gets second Ult
  • When Turbulence procs (+15 Energy each FUA), Feixiao can Ult back-to-back without relying on Tingyun
