Before Dawn — Light Cone Guide

[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Before Dawn is Jing Yuan's signature light cone for the Erudition path. It increases CRIT DMG and boosts follow-up attack and skill damage — perfect for any Erudition character who relies on follow-up attacks.

Best Characters

  • Jing Yuan: Made for him — boosts Lightning Lord damage significantly
  • Himeko: Follow-up attacks benefit from the passive
  • Herta: Kuru-kuru gets a nice damage bump
  • Qingque: Skill damage boost is relevant

Is It Worth Pulling?

If you main Jing Yuan, absolutely. For other Erudition characters, there are better options. The Herta shop light cone "Eternal Calculus" is a solid F2P alternative.
