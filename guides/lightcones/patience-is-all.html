Patience Is All You Need — Light Cone Guide

[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Patience Is All You Need is Kafka's signature Nihility light cone. It applies an extra DOT (Erosion) and increases the wearer's damage against debuffed enemies. It's the cornerstone of any DOT team.

Best Characters

  • Kafka: Perfect synergy — extra DOT = more detonation damage
  • Black Swan: The Erosion DOT stacks with Arcana beautifully
  • Sampo: Solid alternative if you have it but no Kafka
