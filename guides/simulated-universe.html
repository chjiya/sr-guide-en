1. Overview & How It Works

The Simulated Universe (SU) is a roguelike game mode unlocked after completing Trailblaze Level 22. You assemble a team of 4 characters, then run through procedurally generated domains, collecting Blessings (passive buffs tied to a chosen Path), Curios (unique artifacts with powerful effects — and sometimes drawbacks), and Cosmic Fragments (currency for rerolls and upgrades).

Each run culminates in a boss fight against a simulated version of an Elite or Stagnant Shadow enemy. The deeper you go, the harder the enemies — but the better the rewards.

Core Mechanics

  • Path Selection — Before starting, choose one of 9 Paths. Your Path determines which Blessings appear more frequently and what your 3-resonance Path ability does.
  • Blessings — Stackable buffs. Collecting 3/6/10 of your Path's Blessings unlocks Resonance formations and buffs your Path Resonance ability.
  • Curios — One-of-a-kind items. Some are strictly beneficial; others have trade-offs ("double-edged" curios).
  • Cosmic Fragments — Spend at shops to buy Blessings, Curios, or remove negative Curios.
  • Respite Zones — Heal, revive a fallen character, or enhance Blessings (3 upgrades per run).

2. All 9 Paths — Ranked & Explained

Paths are ranked S through B: S (meta-defining), A (strong in the right team), B (niche or power-crept).

S The Hunt — Single-Target Dominance

Best for: Boss kills, Simulated Universe boss floors, any run with a strong main DPS.

The Hunt massively amplifies single-target damage. Its Path Resonance deals devastating ST damage scaling with SPD. Core Blessings give SPD boosts, action advance, and bonus damage against slowed enemies. The 6-Blessing Resonance (Bow and Arrow) fires a chain of ST hits that clean up adds while melting the main target.

Synergy: Seele, Dr. Ratio, Boothill, Feixiao, Topaz.

S Destruction — HP Scaling & Massive AoE

Best for: High-difficulty content, survival + damage hybrid, teams with HP fluctuation.

Destruction Blessings scale off max HP and the party's missing HP. The Path Resonance detonates HP-based AoE damage and heals the team proportionally. Its 10-Blessing "Atomic" upgrade gives massive damage reduction at low HP and retaliatory nukes. Arguably the strongest all-around Path for high-level SU and Swarm Disaster.

Synergy: Blade, Mydei, Jingliu, Firefly, Clara, Yunli.

A Nihility — DoT Amplification

Best for: DoT teams (Kafka, Black Swan, Sampo, Guinafen, Jiaoqiu).

Nihility Blessings make DoTs tick harder, faster, and spread between enemies. Key Blessings increase DoT damage per stack, cause DoTs to crit, and make enemies take bonus damage for each debuff active. Path Resonance applies all DoT types to all enemies at once. The 6-Blessing Resonance (Dewdrop) stores DoT damage and detonates it again.

Synergy: Kafka, Black Swan, Sampo, Guinafen, Jiaoqiu.

A Erudition — AoE Specialization

Best for: Pure Fiction-style content, mob-heavy floors, teams with multiple AoE DPS.

Erudition boosts every multi-target attack. Its Blessings give extra DMG% per enemy hit, energy on multi-target kills, and stacking AoE multipliers. Path Resonance deals damage to all enemies equal to a portion of the team's total energy. Best paired with dedicated AoE carries.

Synergy: Argenti, Herta, Himeko, Jade, Rappa, The Herta.

A Preservation — Shield Stacking

Best for: Survival-oriented runs, low-investment teams, sustain-free comps.

Preservation Blessings turn shields into your primary damage source. Key Blessings cause the team to deal bonus damage equal to a % of current shield value, reflect damage on block, and charge Path Resonance from shield absorption. The 6-Blessing Resonance deals AoE damage scaling with total party shields.

Synergy: Aventurine, Fu Xuan, Gepard, March 7th (Preservation), Trailblazer (Preservation).

A Abundance — Healing & Cleanse

Best for: Sustained fights, teams without dedicated healers, debuff-heavy encounters.

Abundance turns overhealing into damage and provides constant cleanse. Key Blessings add damage to heals, let healing overflow as bonus damage, and auto-cleanse debuffs when HP drops below 50%. Path Resonance is a massive team-wide heal + cleanse that stored a portion of healing done since last use.

Synergy: Luocha, Huohuo, Bailu, Natasha, Lynx, Gallagher.

B Remembrance — Freeze & Dissociation

Best for: Freeze-centric teams, crowd control strategies.

Remembrance focuses on the Freeze debuff and the Dissociation mechanic — a special state that deals bonus damage when it ends. Key Blessings increase freeze chance, extend freeze duration, and cause Dissociation to spread. Path Resonance freezes all enemies and applies Dissociation. Falls off against freeze-immune bosses.

Synergy: Jingliu, Gepard, Herta (freeze synergy), March 7th.

B Propagation — Skill Point Spam

Best for: Skill-point-hungry teams, multi-action carries.

Propagation rewards spamming Skills every turn. Blessings refund skill points on kill, boost Basic ATK damage after skill use, and give SPD per skill point spent. Path Resonance consumes all remaining skill points to deal damage per point. Very powerful in ideal team configurations but narrow.

Synergy: Dan Heng • Imbibitor Lunae, Qingque, Argenti (SP-heavy playstyle).

B Elation — Follow-Up Attack Focus

Best for: FUA-centric teams (Topaz + Numby, Dr. Ratio, Clara, Yunli, Aventurine, Feixiao).

Elation amplifies follow-up attacks. Key Blessings boost FUA DMG, add extra hits per FUA, and give energy on FUA. Path Resonance triggers the party's most recent FUA again at reduced damage. Strong on paper but requires a dedicated FUA team to outpace Destruction or Hunt.

Synergy: Topaz, Dr. Ratio, Clara, Yunli, Aventurine, Feixiao, Jing Yuan, Herta.

5. Domain Routing Strategy

Each SU floor presents a choice of domains. Choosing the right route can make or break a run.

Domain Types

DomainIconBest For
Combat⚔️Early-game Blessing building. 3-star Blessings drop from Elite Combats.
Reward🎁Choosing a specific 1-star or 2-star Blessing. Good for filling your 3/6/10 resonance counts.
Adventure🌟Mini-games for large Cosmic Fragment payouts. Prioritize when saving for shop.
Shop (Respite)🛒Buy/sell Blessings, buy Curios, remove negative Curios. Best domain in the game.
Occurrence❓Random events. High variance. Can give Curios or free Blessings; can also curse you.
Elite💀Tough fight for 3-star Blessing + Curio. Take when your team is strong.
Boss👑Final floor of each plane. Rewards a Curio + high-rarity Blessing.

Routing Priorities by Phase

First Plane (Domains 1–3)

Priority: Combat > Adventure > Reward > Occurrence > Elite

  • Your team is weak. Take safe Combat domains for Blessing collection.
  • Adventure domains give fragments, letting you shop on Plane 2.
  • Avoid Elite if your team isn't built yet.

Second Plane (Domains 4–6)

Priority: Shop > Elite > Adventure > Reward > Occurrence

  • If you saved fragments, the Shop lets you buy missing key Blessings and Curios.
  • If your build is coming together, take Elites for 3-star Blessings.
  • Avoid Occurrences — the risk-reward isn't worth it by now.

Third Plane (Domains 7–9, Boss)

Priority: Elite > Shop > Boss

  • Stack your final Blessings from Elites.
  • Use the last Shop to reroll for 3-star upgrades.
  • Enter the Boss with as close to 10 Path Blessings as possible.

Path-Specific Routing Tips

  • Hunt / Destruction: Prioritize Elites for 3-star Blessings. Both paths spike hardest at 6 and 10 Blessings.
  • Nihility: Reward domains are excellent for hitting 3/6 breakpoints. You need the specific DoT-crit Blessing.
  • Preservation / Abundance: You can facetank Elites early. Take more Elites than other paths.
  • Remembrance / Propagation / Elation: Spec into Occurrences for the off-chance of rare unique Blessings that make these paths work.

6. Expansion Modes

Swarm Disaster

The first major SU expansion. Adds a secondary Path mechanic — you equip two Paths and can use both Resonance abilities. The Swarm enemy type features bugs that multiply and explode.

  • Communication: Use the "Swarm Communicator" to unlock story segments and hidden bosses.
  • Best Paths: Destruction + Abundance for survival. Destruction + Hunt for speed clears. Nihility + Elation for DoT + FUA hybrid.
  • Hardest Boss: True Sting (Swarm: Golden Sting). Bring high AoE to clear adds.
  • Rewards: Stellar Jade, self-modeling resin, exclusive Light Cone (expansion pass).

Gold & Gears

The second expansion, centered on custom Dice. Each die face can be customized with effects that trigger when you land on that domain type.

  • Dice Customization: Use Wissen (knowledge currency) earned by completing runs to unlock new die faces. Build dice that favor your chosen Path.
  • Best Dice Builds: For Hunt — faces that grant extra combat domains. For Preservation — faces that boost shield-related rewards.
  • Hardest Boss: Argenti (Gold & Gears version) — massive AoE spam. Bring Preservation or Abundance sustain.
  • Rewards: Stellar Jade, Dice materials, exclusive Light Cone (expansion pass).

Divergent Universe

The latest (and current) expansion. Replaces Blessing tiers with Equations — pre-built blessing combos that activate when you collect a certain number of related Blessings.

  • Equations: Each Path has multiple Equations. Completing an Equation's requirement (e.g., "5 Hunt Blessings + 3 Nihility Blessings") unlocks powerful set bonuses. Plan your Blessing picks around 1-2 Equations.
  • Weighted Curios: New Curios that scale in power based on rarity. Some have "weight" requirements (e.g., "Equip 3 Nihility Blessings" to activate).
  • Best Strategies: Focus on 1 primary Path's Equation + 1 secondary Path's Equation. Don't spread picks thin.
  • Hardest Boss: Varies by rotation. Check the current Divergent Universe buff schedule.
  • Rewards: 8,000 weekly points max for Stellar Jade + Planar Ornament materials.

7. Team Building for SU

General-Purpose Team (Any Path)

A balanced composition that works with any Path and any expansion mode:

  • Main DPS — Your primary damage dealer (e.g., Jingliu, Seele, Dr. Ratio, Firefly).
  • Sub-DPS / Support — Provides buffs, debuffs, or secondary damage. (e.g., Ruan Mei, Robin, Sunday, Tingyun, Sparkle).
  • Sustain — Healer or shielder. (e.g., Aventurine, Luocha, Huohuo, Gallagher, Fu Xuan).
  • Flex — Second support or sub-DPS depending on the Path. (e.g., Jiaoqiu for Nihility, Topaz for Elation).

Team Building Tips

  • Don't over-invest in sustain in base SU. Bring one sustain (healer or shielder) and let Blessings handle the rest.
  • In Swarm Disaster and high-diff Gold & Gears, dual sustain (healer + shielder) may be necessary until your Blessing collection is online.
  • Harmony characters are universally strong. Ruan Mei, Robin, Sunday, and Sparkle work in almost any team.
  • Weakness Break matching matters in SU. Bring elements that match the boss's weaknesses. The boss on floor 9 is fixed per SU cycle — plan ahead.
  • SP management — If you're running Propagation or a Skill-heavy team, include a SP-positive support (Sparkle, Huohuo, or Tingyun).

8. Weekly Reward Optimization

Base SU — Points Per Run

Cap is 10,000 points per week. Higher difficulties give much more efficient points-per-run. Run the highest difficulty you can comfortably clear in 15–20 minutes.

Divergent Universe — Weekly Cap

8,000 points per week. Same principle: run the highest difficulty available. Divergent Universe runs tend to be faster than base SU (~10–15 minutes) due to the Equation system.

Optimization Strategy

  • Day 1: Clear your highest base SU world once (~3,000–4,500 pts). Do 1 Divergent Universe run (~2,000–3,000 pts).
  • Day 2: Another base SU run to cap or near-cap. Another DU run.
  • Don't waste runs: If you're 2,000 points from cap and your run gives 4,000, you lose 2,000 points. Run lower difficulty to avoid overflow.
  • One-time rewards: Swarm Disaster and Gold & Gears don't have a weekly cap — they reward once per difficulty clear. Prioritize these if you haven't cleared them. Each cleared difficulty gives Stellar Jade, often 200–300 per clear.
