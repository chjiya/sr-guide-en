🌌 Simulated Universe Overview

The Simulated Universe (SU) is HSR's roguelike endgame mode featuring randomized Domains, Path selection, Blessings, Curios, and escalating boss fights. Each run has 8-15 Domains across three Planes. Mastering Path synergy, Blessing prioritization, and Domain routing is essential for clearing high-difficulty content like Swarm Disaster V, Gold & Gears Conundrum 12, and Divergent Universe Protocol 8.

Three expansion modes add unique mechanics: Swarm Disaster (secondary Path system), Gold & Gears (custom Dice faces), and Divergent Universe (Equation synergies). Each has its own progression ladder and reward structure.

🎯 Path-by-Path Blessing Guide

🦅 The Hunt

Path Resonance — "Bow and Arrow": Massive single-target damage scaling with Hunt Blessings collected.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Borehole: +30% Crit Rate after using Supremacy. Non-negotiable core blessing.
  • Incision: 20% action advance after attack. Pairs with Seele's Resets for infinite turns.

Best Characters: Seele (resets trigger Incision constantly), Feixiao, Jingliu, Bronya, Topaz.

💥 Destruction

Path Resonance — "Godfather": HP-sacrifice AoE damage scaling with max HP.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Drought: Consume 40% HP to deal 100% Max HP AoE damage — the single strongest blessing in the game.
  • Sustenance (2★): Heal 20% HP per action while above 50% HP. Required for survivability.

Best Characters: Blade (HP-scaling god), Mydei (new v3.x HP DPS), Jingliu (self-HP drain), Fuxuan.

🎭 Elation

Path Resonance — "Kleio's Approval": FUA damage steroid + post-FUA burst.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Joy: After every 6 FUA, next FUA deals +200% DMG. Cornerstone of this path.
  • Drama (2★): 65% chance to recover 1 SP per FUA. Infinite SP for FUA teams.

Best Characters: Topaz & Numby (king of Elation), Feixiao, Dr. Ratio, Yunli, Clara, Aventurine (his FUA procs everything).

🧬 Propagation

Path Resonance — "Spore Burst": AoE damage consuming Skill Points.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Spore: Basic ATK regenerates 1 SP. Infinite SP engine.
  • Overflow (2★): +40% DMG when SP > 5.

Best Characters: DHIL (3 SP/turn), Qingque, Aglaea, Sparkle (SP battery).

☠️ Nihility

Path Resonance — "Withering": Stacking DoT damage.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Gallop: All DoT on an enemy is immediately triggered after taking DoT. Kafka's dream blessing.
  • Funeral (2★): +25% DoT damage per debuff stack.

Best Characters: Kafka (Gallop triggers all DoTs instantly), Black Swan, Sampo, Guinaifen.

❄️ Remembrance

Path Resonance — "Absolute Zero": Dissociation debuff detonates for massive Ice DMG.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Perfect Experience: Dissociation triggers every 3rd attack instead of 6th — doubles damage output.
  • Chill (2★): 60% Freeze chance per attack.

Best Characters: Jingliu, Yanqing, Gepard, March 7th, Herta.

📚 Erudition

Path Resonance — "Infinite Intellect": Chaining AoE damage.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Contemplation: +60% / +120% Ultimate DMG vs 2+ / 5+ enemies.
  • Enlightenment (2★): Ultimate ignores 20% DEF.

Best Characters: Argenti (Erudition king), Herta, Himeko, Jing Yuan, Serval.

🛡️ Preservation

Path Resonance — "Ishmael's Chant": Shield-scaling damage.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Macrosegregation: After taking DMG, gain shield = 80% DEF. Infinite shields.
  • Construct (1★): +35% shield strength.

Best Characters: Aventurine (stacking FUA shields), Fuxuan, Gepard, Fire MC.

💚 Abundance

Path Resonance — "Anatta": Heal + overhealing-based damage.

Key 3★ Blessings:

  • Anatta Communication: Remove all debuffs on Anatta use.
  • Resurgence (2★): Cleanse 1 debuff when healed to full.

Best Characters: Luocha (auto-heal field), Huohuo, Bailu, Lynx.

🗺️ Expansion Mode Strategies

🪲 Swarm Disaster V

Mechanic: Communication — choose a secondary Path. Difficulty V adds Propagation-based final boss.

Best Strategy: Remembrance (main) + Elation (secondary). Dissociation from Remembrance stops Swarm attacks, while Elation FUA handles the adds.

Team: Jingliu + Bronya + Ruan Mei + Huohuo. Freeze-chain perma-locks the Swarm True Sting.

Domain Priority: Occurrence > Combat (for Blessings) > Reward > Respite. Take as many Occurrence Domains as possible for the extra Planar Ornaments and unique Swarm Dice.

⚙️ Gold & Gears Conundrum 12

Mechanic: Custom Dice faces roll after each Domain. Conundrum 12 gives enemies 5x HP and 2.5x ATK, strips 6 Blessing slots.

Best Strategy: Destruction (main) + Elation (secondary). Destruction's HP-sacrifice damage scales past the HP multiplier. Elation provides additional damage through FUA.

Best Dice Face: "Company Time" — doubles all Blessing effects for 3 Domains. Reroll aggressively for it.

Team: Blade / Mydei + Bronya + Ruan Mei + Luocha. HP-scaling DPS are mandatory at Conundrum 12.

🌀 Divergent Universe Protocol 8

Mechanic: Equations replace Path Resonance. Pre-defined Blessing synergies auto-activate when you collect enough matching Blessings. Protocol 8 enemies act twice per turn and ignore 50% of non-Crit DMG reduction.

Best Equations:

  • "Beneath the Old Oak" (Elation + Hunt): FUA + Crit — best for Feixiao/Ratio teams.
  • "Blood Debt" (Destruction + Nihility): HP sacrifice + DoT — Blade/Mydei with Black Swan.
  • "Frost Moon" (Remembrance + Erudition): Freeze chain + AoE — Jingliu + Herta.

Strategy: Focus on completing one Equation as fast as possible. Equations provide exponentially more power than individual Blessings. Reroll at Respite domains to finish your chosen Equation.

🏛️ Domain Routing Strategy

  • Plane 1 (Domains 1-3): Combat → Combat → Reward. Build your core Blessing foundation. Prioritize 1★ and 2★ Blessings for your main Path.
  • Plane 2 (Domains 4-6): Elite → Occurrence → Respite. The Elite domain drops higher-tier Blessings. Visit the Respite to upgrade your best 3★ Blessing or reroll.
  • Plane 3 (Domains 7-9): Combat → Elite → Boss. Fill remaining slots before the final boss. Your build should be 80%+ complete by now.
  • Occurrence Domains: Take these when you have 100+ Cosmic Fragments — the Merchant Respite lets you buy 3★ Blessings.
  • Save 150+ Fragments: For the Plane 2 Merchant. You can buy one 3★ Blessing for ~120 Fragments.

    📌 General SU Tips

    1. Blessing Synergy > Quantity: 10 synergistic Blessings outperform 30 random ones. Focus your picks on completing your Path's 3★ core.
    2. Cosmic Fragments: Save 100+ for the Plane 2 Merchant. Buying a 3★ Blessing is the most efficient use of Fragments.
    3. Reroll Strategically: Always reroll at the first Respite. If your Path's key 3★ isn't appearing, pivot to a strong secondary Path.
    4. Path Resonance Cheat: If your damage is falling off, add a secondary Path that complements your weakness (e.g., Preservation main → Nihility for debuffs).
    5. Planar Ornament Farming: SU is the only source for Planar sets. Divergent Universe Protocol 8 gives the best returns per run.
    6. Boss Rage Phase: Most SU bosses hit harder below 30% HP. Save Resonance + Ults for this window to burst through.
    7. Curio Risk: Skip negative Curios unless you're confident in your build. A bad Curio can tank an otherwise perfect run.
    8. Secondary Path in DU: In Divergent Universe, your Equation determines your playstyle. Pick your Equation at the start and tailor all Blessing picks toward completing it.
