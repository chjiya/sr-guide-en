🎯 Simulated Universe Overview

Simulated Universe (SU) is Honkai: Star Rail's permanent roguelike mode. Each run you choose a Path (determines your special Path Resonance ability and blessing pool), collect blessings (boosts), and fight escalating battles across multiple planes. SU is the primary source for Planar Ornaments (relics) and provides a significant portion of Stellar Jade income through first-time clears and weekly point rewards.

Key Systems (v3.4)

  • 8 Paths: Remembrance, Erudition, Propagation, Elation, Nihility, Abundance, Preservation, Hunt
  • Blessings: 3 tiers (1★, 2★, 3★) with resonance and interpath synergies
  • Equations (Gold & Gears): Pre-built blessing combos that activate powerful effects
  • Curios: Positive/negative artifacts that can dramatically change your run
  • Expansion modes: Gold & Gears, Swarm Disaster, Uncharted Track (v3.4)

🛤️ Complete Path Guide v3.4

❄️ Path of Remembrance — SS Tier for v3.4

The Remembrance path centers on memosprite synergy and dissociation. With v3.4's memosprite meta, this path has become the strongest SU path for accounts with Castorice, Aglaea, or any memosprite DPS.

Best blessings: "Memoria Eternal" (3★ — dissociation on enemy turn start), "From Here to Eternity" (3★ — memosprite action advance), "Vanishing Dreams" (2★ — increased dissociation DMG)

Path Resonance: Deals Ice DMG and applies Dissociation. With 3★ blessings, triggers AOE dissociation chains.

Best teams: Castorice + Sunday, Aglaea + Robin, any memosprite team

📚 Path of Erudition — S Tier

Introduced alongside The Herta, the Erudition path focuses on Interpretation stacks and brain in a vat energy recovery. Each time an enemy takes DoT or is hit by a follow-up, you gain Interpretation stacks that boost all damage by up to 200% at max stacks.

Best blessings: "The Mind's Eye" (3★ — interpretation never resets), "Eureka Moment" (3★ — brain in a vat triggers on every third ultimate), "Knowledge Integration" (2★ — interpretation gained on FUA)

Path Resonance: Deals massive AOE Quantum DMG scaled by Interpretation stacks. Excellent for wave clearing.

Best teams: The Herta hypercarry, Jade + Herta duo, Argenti

🦗 Path of Propagation — S Tier

Propagation focuses on skill point generation and basic attack empowerment. It's the premier path for SP-hungry DPS characters and those with enhanced basic attacks.

Best blessings: "Sporogenesis" (3★ — unlimited skill points after ultimate), "Nourishing Growth" (2★ — basic attack deals enhanced damage), "Reproduction" (1★ — gain SP when entering battle)

Path Resonance: Consumes all remaining SP to deal Quantum DMG per point consumed. Can reach nuke-level damage with high SP generation.

Best teams: Dan Heng IL, Qingque, Seele, Jingliu (skill-heavy DPS)

🎭 Path of Elation — A Tier

Elation amplifies follow-up attacks with massive damage bonuses and additional hits. Very strong in the current Crystalline Storm era.

Best blessings: "Chorus of Many Voices" (3★ — FUA DMG bonus based on hit count), "Encore!" (2★ — chance to repeat FUA), "Backup Dancer" (1★ — FUA increases team SPD)

Path Resonance: Triggers additional FUA across all enemies whenever any ally performs a FUA. Creates cascading FUA chains.

Best teams: Feixiao FUA, Topaz + Numby, Clara + Yunli, Herta + Himeko

🌑 Path of Nihility — A Tier

Nihility focuses on DoT amplification, debuff extension, and enemy susceptibility. The premier path for Kafka, Black Swan, and all DoT specialists.

Best blessings: "The Suspiciousness of the Gourmet" (3★ — all DoTs immediately deal 120% of their damage when inflicted), "The Deathly Illness of the Patient" (2★ — DoT DMG +50%), "The Emptiness of the Blue" (1★ — extended DoT duration)

Path Resonance: Implants all DoT types on all enemies and instantly triggers them once each. Catastrophic burst damage in the right setup.

Best teams: Kafka + Black Swan, Guinaifen + Sampo, any DoT composition

🌿 Path of Abundance — A Tier

Abundance provides sustain, cleansing, and HP-scaling damage. Excellent for survival and for DPS units that scale with max HP.

Best blessings: "Anatta: The Supreme Source of All Things" (3★ — team-wide cleanse + massive heal), "The Cleansing of the Body" (2★ — Anatta triggers automatically on debuff), "Yearning for the Fragrance of Life" (1★ — HP drain converted to healing)

Path Resonance: Heals the entire team and deals damage equal to 30% of the total overhealed HP to all enemies. Scales with team max HP.

Best teams: Blade, Mydei, Fu Xuan (HP scaling), any team needing sustain

🛡️ Path of Preservation — B Tier

Preservation provides shielding, damage reduction, and shield-scaling damage. The safest path for consistent runs but slower clear times.

Best blessings: "Macrosegregation" (3★ — shield strength +100%), "Construction of the Human Body" (2★ — shield refreshes automatically), "Isomorphic Reaction" (1★ — gain shield on turn start)

Best teams: Aventurine hypercarry (damage scaling on DEF), Gepard shield teams

🏹 Path of The Hunt — B Tier

The Hunt provides SPD, action advance, and single-target damage amplification. Strong but focused on single-target scenarios — weaker in AoE-heavy rooms.

Best blessings: "The Killing Blade Turns to Ice" (3★ — killing an enemy action-advances all allies), "The Gash of the Bow" (2★ — SPD buff on kill), "The Quickening of the Bow" (1★ — SPD + turn advancement)

Best teams: Seele, Boothill, Feixiao in single-target boss rooms

🧩 Equation Building (Gold & Gears)

In Gold & Gears mode, Equations are pre-built blessing combinations that activate powerful additional effects when you collect all required blessings. Here are the top equations for v3.4:

💎 "The All-Knowing" (Erudition, 3★ Equation): When an ally uses their Ultimate, all enemies take Quantum DMG equal to 150% of their Interpretation stacks. Best for: The Herta, Argenti, any frequent-ult DPS.
💎 "The Eternal Theater" (Remembrance, 3★ Equation): Memosprites gain 60% action advance every time they attack. Dissociated enemies take 40% increased memosprite DMG. Best for: Castorice, Aglaea, Topaz + Numby.
💎 "The Unending Performance" (Elation, 3★ Equation): Every 3 FUAs trigger a Resonance that hits all enemies. FUA CRIT DMG +100%. Best for: Feixiao FUA team, Clara, Yunli.
💎 "The Infinite Loop" (Propagation + Nihility, Cross-path Equation): Using a Basic Attack consumes 1 SP to trigger all DoTs on the target. Best for: Kafka + Dan Heng IL hybrid teams.

🗺️ Expansion Mode Guides

🥇 Gold & Gears

The flagship SU expansion featuring Equations, Dice rerolling, and the Retirement system. Key tips:

  • Choose your path first: Remembrance and Erudition are the strongest for v3.4
  • Prioritize 3★ equations: A single 3★ equation is worth more than three 1★ equations
  • Dice management: Save your custom dice for the final two planes — the first plane can be cleared without optimal dice
  • Retirement strategy: Retire early (after floor 8-10) when you have a strong equation set for maximum point efficiency

🦗 Swarm Disaster

The Propagation-themed expansion with the Communication and Communing mechanic. Key tips:

  • Always take Propagation blessings: Even if your main path is different, Propagation 1★ blessings (SP generation) are universally useful
  • Difficulty V clear: Use Remembrance with Castorice or Erudition with The Herta for the final boss
  • Swarm boss: The True Sting boss summons Lesser Stings — use blast/AoE attacks to clear them quickly while damaging the main body

🆕 Uncharted Track (v3.4)

The newest expansion mode featuring randomized plane order, mystery events, and the Track system. Key tips:

  • Track selection matters: Choose tracks that match your team's element weaknesses
  • Mystery events: Always pick the mystery option in Curiosity rooms — the rewards (3★ blessings, rare curios) far outweigh the risks
  • Boss tracks: Save your resonance charges for the boss track — the boss has 40% increased HP compared to Gold & Gears

📅 Weekly Point Optimization

SU has a weekly point system that rewards up to 13,000 points per week (previously 10,000). Here's the optimal route:

  1. First clear of the week: Run the highest-difficulty Uncharted Track you can clear. This gives the biggest point multiplier (2.5x).
  2. Second clear: Run Gold & Gears at difficulty IV+ for 2x point multiplier.
  3. Third+ clears: Speed-run Swarm Disaster at difficulty III with a strong FUA team — fastest clears per point ratio.
  4. Daily SU runs: Only necessary if you need Planar Ornament farming. Otherwise, 3 runs per week is sufficient for max points.

❓ FAQ

Q1: Which path is best for Planar Ornament farming?

For speed farming, use Elation with a FUA team (Feixiao + Topaz + Aventurine + Robin) — this clears the fastest. For consistent clearing with lower-geared accounts, use Remembrance (memosprite safety net) or Abundance (never-die sustain).

Q2: Does Uncharted Track replace Gold & Gears?

No — Uncharted Track is an additional mode alongside Gold & Gears and Swarm Disaster. Each mode has separate first-clear Stellar Jade rewards and contributes to the weekly point total. You can and should play all three for maximum rewards.

Q3: What's the best use of Universal Enhancement items?

Universal Enhancement items should be saved for 3★ blessings of your chosen primary path. Never use them on 1★ blessings — the upgrade cost is the same but the power gain is much smaller. In Gold & Gears, also consider using them on key Equation pieces if you're one blessing away from completing a 3★ equation.

Q4: How do I unlock all ending bosses?

Each expansion mode has different bosses based on your chosen path and progression level. In Gold & Gears, path-specific bosses unlock at Knowledge Level milestones. In Swarm Disaster, you unlock bosses by completing Communing tiers and defeating the Swarm Lord at increasing difficulties. The final boss of Uncharted Track is random but weighted toward bosses whose elements match your team's weaknesses (if you have multiple units sharing an element).
