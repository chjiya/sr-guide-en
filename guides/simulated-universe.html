🌌 SU Overview

Simulated Universe (SU) is Honkai: Star Rail's rogue-lite mode. You take a team through procedurally generated domains, collecting blessings, curios, and path resonances to progressively empower your team. SU rewards range from weekly Stellar Jade (up to 225 per week from points) to exclusive Light Cones, materials, and credits. Clearing higher worlds unlocks more paths and story content.

SU has evolved significantly since launch. The base mode (Worlds 1-9) serves as an introduction, while the expansion modes — Swarm Disaster, Gold and Gears, and Divergent Universe — add deeper mechanics and higher difficulty tiers. Each expansion mode introduces unique mechanics and rewards that make them worth revisiting monthly.

💎 Must-Pick Blessings by Path

Each path has key blessings that dramatically boost your team's power. Here are the essential pickups:

Hunt

  • Top Blessing: "Bow and Arrow" — After a character uses an Ultimate, their next attack deals 60% more DMG. Combines with Hunt resonance for massive burst.
  • Second Priority: "Backlight" — Killing an enemy advances the team's action forward by 16%. Snowballs into infinite turns on add-heavy floors.
  • Key Curio: "Divine Flame" — +35% CRIT DMG. Standard Hunt BiS curio.

Destruction

  • Top Blessing: "Extreme Evasion" (3-star) — +80% ATK when under 35% HP, and characters resist lethal damage once per battle.
  • Second Priority: "Burning Ambition" — When HP is reduced, deal extra DMG equal to 50% of the lost HP as True DMG.

Nihility (DoT)

  • Top Blessing: "Emotional Sharing" — When a DoT is applied, there's a 65% chance to apply 1 extra stack. Critical for Kafka/Black Swan ceiling.
  • Second Priority: "Muddled Feather" — DoT can CRIT. This single blessing transforms DoT damage output.

Remembrance (Freeze)

  • Top Blessing: "Perfect Experience: Innocence" — Dissociation (Remembrance's special Freeze) deals 50% true DMG to frozen enemies when the freeze ends.
  • Second Priority: "Chilling Freeze" — All allies' Ultimate DMG increases by 30%. Frozen enemies take 25% more DMG.

Preservation

  • Top Blessing: "Macrosegregation" (3-star) — All shields are 45% stronger. Simple, universal, enormous for Aventurine and Fu Xuan.
  • Second Priority: "Construct: Surge" — When shield value is refreshed, deal 100% of shield strength as DMG to enemies.

🧩 Expansion Modes

🐜 Swarm Disaster

Swarm Disaster is the first major SU expansion, centered on fighting the Swarm (propagation of bugs). It introduces the Propagation path and a new map system with branching paths, skill trees, and a persistent upgrade system (Communicating Cocoons).

Key Mechanics:

  • Skill Tree: Invest points into permanent upgrades that carry between runs. Maxing the skill tree is essential for the highest difficulty tiers.
  • Propagation Path: This exclusive path synergizes with basic attack spam and SP generation — ideal for Dan Heng IL, Qingque, and Seele.
  • Difficulty Levels: Range from I to V (added in later patches). Level V is the hardest PvE content in the game and requires optimized teams and full path resonance.
  • Bosses: You face the Swarm: True Sting (a swarm Propagation-aspected boss) at the end, with different variations depending on the path you choose.

Recommended Team: Fu Xuan (sustain) + Ruan Mei (support) + Kafka (flex DoT) + any main DPS (preferably AoE). The Swarm love to summon adds, so AoE is critical.

⚙️ Gold and Gears

Gold and Gears is the second expansion, themed around IPC/Screwllum. It introduces the Erudition path, custom dice mechanics, and a more strategic map navigation system.

Key Mechanics:

  • Custom Dice: You build a dice with up to 5 faces (upgradable), each with special effects. Face types include Combat (more battles for more buffs), Adventure (curio-focused), Occurrence (event-focused), and Boss (boss-focused for double rewards).
  • Company Investments: IPC quasi-currency you earn during runs. Spend it on blessings, curios, or resurrection in the shops.
  • Difficulty Tiers: Upto difficulty V with Conundrum levels (additional debuff conditions) that dramatically increase challenge.
  • Unique Rewards: This mode is the source of the S-rank Simulated Universe Light Cones (Herta's store update). Clear for exclusive currency.

Recommended Team: Jingliu/Bronya hypercarry or a well-built DoT team. The dice system lets you tailor runs to specific archetypes. Focus on getting a good die face that matches your playstyle.

🌀 Divergent Universe

Divergent Universe (DU) is the newest SU expansion mode. It streamlines the SU experience with faster runs, a new Equation system that replaces traditional blessing pick-ups, and higher weekly reward caps.

Key Mechanics:

  • Equations: Instead of picking individual blessings, you collect Equations — pre-built blessing combos that activate powerful effects when you have enough blessings from the associated paths. Equations are the primary power system in DU.
  • Resonance Extrapolation: Extends your path resonance with additional effects based on how many Equations you've completed.
  • Weighted Curio System: Curio drops are weighted toward synergistic options rather than fully random.
  • Shorter Runs: A typical DU run takes 15-20 minutes, compared to 30-45 for base SU or Gold and Gears.

Recommended Team: Build around a specific Equation archetype. For example, the "DoT Equation" works best with Kafka + Black Swan; the "Break Equation" works with Firefly + Ruan Mei; the "FUA Equation" with Feixiao + Topaz + Robin. Check which Equation is available at the start of your run and build your team around it for maximum efficiency.

💡 Efficient Farming Strategy

  • Weekly SU Points: Cap is 225 Stellar Jade per week (accumulated from weekly points earned through SU runs). The fastest way to cap is 2-3 runs of Divergent Universe (15-20 min each) in the current v3.4 cycle.
  • Planar Ornaments: SU is the only source of Planar Ornament sets. For your main DPS, farm the latest Ornament set (Duran, Firmament Frontline, or the v3.4 sets). Prioritize this once your main traces are level 8+.
  • Swarm Disaster Cycling: Each world completion in Swarm Disaster gives ~600 Stellar Jade one-time (first clear). Complete all worlds for the one-time polychrome before focusing on weekly farming.
  • Gold and Gears Unlocks: Don't rush to max difficulty immediately. Work through the skill tree and dice upgrades first — the incremental upgrades make a dramatic difference.
  • Resource Conversion: After maxing your Planar Ornament sets, use Immersion Rewards (the purple SU currency) to purchase relic remains, which convert to self-modeling resin in the relic crafting system.

❓ FAQ

What's the minimum team to clear SU World 8+?

Worlds 8+ require level 70+ characters with leveled traces and +12 relics. The key is having a competent sustain (any Preservation or Abundance character built properly). A healless team (3 DPS + 1 support) can clear lower worlds but struggles with World 7+. Recommended: 1 sustain + 1 harmony/nihility support + 1-2 DPS. With good blessings, even a suboptimal team can clear — blessings, not character quality, are the primary determinant of SU success.

How do I unlock Propagation path?

The Propagation path is unlocked through Swarm Disaster progression. Complete Swarm Disaster's first few difficulty tiers and the path becomes available in all SU modes. It's the newest path added and can only be unlocked via Swarm Disaster progression.

Which SU expansion should I do first?

Start with base SU Worlds 1-8 to unlock all basic paths. Then move to Divergent Universe for the fastest weekly farming — it's the most time-efficient for reaching the Stellar Jade cap. Tackle Swarm Disaster next for the one-time Stellar Jade rewards and Propagation path. Finally, do Gold and Gears if you enjoy deep strategy and want the exclusive Light Cones from Herta's store.

Can I use the same team in SU that I use in MoC?

Generally yes, with some adjustments. MoC teams are built for speed and burst damage. SU teams should prioritize sustainability and synergy with blessings. For example, a MoC hypercarry team (Jingliu/Bronya/Pela) works in SU but benefits from replacing Pela with a sustain like Fu Xuan or Huohuo. The key difference: in MoC you kill enemies before they act; in SU, enemies often survive multiple cycles, so survivability matters more.
