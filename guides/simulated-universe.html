🌌 What Is the Simulated Universe?

The Simulated Universe (SU) is HSR's roguelike endgame mode where you assemble a team, choose a Path, collect Blessings and Curios across randomized Domains, then face a final boss. Your Path determines your Path Resonance (an ultimate-like ability) and Resonance Formations (passive bonuses). The mode features three major expansion modes — Swarm Disaster, Gold & Gears, and Divergent Universe — each with unique mechanics and escalating difficulty.

Each run has 8-15 Domains depending on the expansion mode. You encounter Combat, Elite, Occurrence, Reward (Respite), Abundance (for Patch-specific events), and Boss Domains. Mastering Domain routing and Blessing synergy is the key to clearing high-difficulty content.

🎯 Path-by-Path Breakdown

🦅 The Hunt

Path Resonance: "Bow and Arrow" — Deals massive single-target damage based on the number of Hunt Blessings collected. The Resonance gains bonus Crit Rate and Crit DMG, making it one of the highest-damage resonances in the game.

Best Blessings:

  • Borehole (3★): Increases Crit Rate by 30% after using Supremecy. Mandatory for Hunt runs.
  • Incision (3★): After a character attacks, advances their action forward by 20%. Pairs perfectly with Seele / Bronya.
  • Shieldbreaker (2★): Increases DMG dealt to Elite enemies by 35%.
  • Ambush (2★): After Ultimate use, next attack has 100% Crit Rate.

Best Characters: Seele (god-tier with Hunt — her Resets trigger constant action advances), Jingliu, Feixiao, Topaz, Dr. Ratio, Bronya (essential buffer).

💥 Destruction

Path Resonance: "Godfather" — HP-sacrifice mechanic that deals proportional damage to all enemies. Scales off max HP, making high-HP characters ideal.

Best Blessings:

  • Drought (3★): Consume 40% HP to deal 100% Max HP damage to all enemies. One of the most powerful blessings in the game.
  • Sustenance (2★): Heal 20% of HP after each action while HP is above 50%.
  • Reeves (2★): Deal 50% more DMG when HP percentage is lower than the enemy's.
  • Pale Fire (1★): Increase ATK based on lost HP.

Best Characters: Blade (HP-scaling synergy), Clara, Jingliu, Arlan, Fu Xuan (her CC prevents lethal damage).

🎭 Elation

Path Resonance: "Kleio's Approval" — Deals damage after every FUA and provides a massive crit steroid for follow-up attacks.

Best Blessings:

  • Joy (3★): After every 6 FUA, the next FUA deals 200% more DMG. The cornerstone Elation blessing.
  • Drama (2★): FUA has 65% chance to recover 1 Skill Point.
  • Mascot (1★): FUA deals 55% more DMG.
  • Deception (2★): Increase CRIT Rate of FUA by 30%.

Best Characters: Topaz & Numby (king of Elation), Dr. Ratio, Clara, Yunli, Herta, Himeko, Aventurine (his FUA triggers everything).

🧬 Propagation

Path Resonance: "Spore Burst" — Consumes Skill Points to deal AoE damage that scales with the number of Skill Points spent in battle.

Best Blessings:

  • Spore (3★): After using Basic ATK, regenerate 1 Skill Point. Unlimited SP for Qingque/DHIL.
  • Overflow (2★): When Skill Points exceed 5, gain 40% DMG boost.
  • Nourishment (1★): Gain 1 extra Skill Point at the start of battle.
  • Breed (1★): Basic ATK deals 60% more DMG.

Best Characters: Dan Heng IL (consumes 3 SP per turn), Qingque (gambler's paradise), Seele, Silver Wolf, Sparkle (SP battery).

☠️ Nihility

Path Resonance: "Withering" — Stacking DoT that deals damage based on the number of debuffs on enemies. Excellently scales with Blessings that amplify DoT damage.

Best Blessings:

  • Gallop (3★): After enemy takes DoT, all DoTs on that enemy are immediately triggered. Kafka's dream blessing.
  • Funeral (2★): Enemy takes 25% increased DoT damage per stack of debuff.
  • Lethargy (2★): Enemy SPD reduced by 15% per DoT. Excellent survivability.
  • Lingering (1★): DoT lasts 1 extra turn.

Best Characters: Kafka (Gallop proc machine), Black Swan, Sampo, Guinaifen, Ruan Mei (her break extension pairs with DoT stacking).

❄️ Remembrance

Path Resonance: "Absolute Zero" — Applies the Dissociation debuff which detonates for massive Ice DMG after a number of hits. Dissociation is a separate multiplier that bypasses normal damage limits.

Best Blessings:

  • Perfect Experience (3★): Dissociation triggers on every 3rd attack instead of 6th. Doubles the damage output of this Path.
  • Chill (2★): 60% chance to Freeze an enemy on hit for 1 turn.
  • Memory (1★): Dissociation deals 35% more DMG.
  • Bittering (1★): When enemy emerges from Freeze, take 30% more DMG.

Best Characters: Jingliu, Yanqing, Gepard (his freeze application helps), Welt (imprison pairs with freeze), March 7th, Herta.

📚 Erudition

Path Resonance: "Infinite Intellect" — Deals AoE damage that spreads between enemies, chaining and ramping. Best for multi-target floors in SU.

Best Blessings:

  • Contemplation (3★): Increases Ultimate DMG by 60% against 2+ enemies and by 120% against 5+ enemies.
  • Inspiration (2★): 50% chance to recover 1 SP after using Ultimate.
  • Resonance (1★): Increases Path Resonance DMG by 75% against adjacent enemies.
  • Enlightenment (2★): Ultimate ignores 20% of enemy DEF.

Best Characters: Argenti (king of Erudition), Himeko, Herta (double hits), Jing Yuan (Lightning Lord counts separately), Serval.

🛡️ Preservation

Path Resonance: "Ishmael's Chant" — Deals damage based on total party shields. Scales with shield strength, making Preservation a surprisingly offensive path with the right Blessings.

Best Blessings:

  • Macrosegregation (3★): After taking DMG, gain a shield equal to 80% of DEF. Infinite shields with 3★ DEF scaling.
  • Reeves II (2★): When Shield is active, take 30% less DMG.
  • Construct (1★): Increase shield strength by 35%.
  • Isolation (2★): Characters take 80% less DMG while shielded.

Best Characters: Fu Xuan (tank + HP share), Gepard (massive shield), Aventurine (stacking shields with FUA), Trailblazer (Fire, free shields).

💚 Abundance

Path Resonance: "Anatta" — Massive team-wide heal plus splash damage based on overhealing. The damage portion makes Abundance viable for offensive play in Dolores-heavy bosses.

Best Blessings:

  • Anatta Communication (3★): Remove all debuffs from allies when Anatta is used.
  • Resurgence (2★): When an ally is healed to full HP, cleanse 1 debuff.
  • Pulse (1★): Heal when allies attack.
  • Fertility (2★): Increase Max HP by 25%.

Best Characters: Luocha (auto-heal field), Huohuo (team energy + heal), Bailu (revive), Natasha, Lynx.

🗺️ Expansion Modes

🪲 Swarm Disaster

Swarm Disaster adds the Communication mechanic — you can choose a second Path to supplement your main Path's Blessings. Difficulty increases through the Swarm Disaster Difficulty V ladder, introducing Propagation-based bosses and the Swarm True Sting final boss.

Swarm Disaster V Tips: Take Remembrance + Elation as your main + secondary Paths. The Dissociation from Remembrance stops the Swarm's devastating skill spam, while Elation handles ads through FUA. Prioritize Occurrence Domains for extra Planar Ornaments and the unique Swarm Dice.

Best Team: Jingliu + Bronya + Ruan Mei + Huohuo (Remembrance main). The constant freeze chain stops the Swarm from acting entirely.

⚙️ Gold & Gears

Gold & Gears introduces the Dice system — custom dice faces that roll after each Domain to provide bonuses. Conundrum Level 12 is the highest difficulty, where enemies gain massive stat multipliers and players lose Blessing slots.

💡 Gold & Gears Conundrum 12 Strategy: Use Destruction + Nihility dual Paths. Destruction's HP-sacrifice damage scales past the massive enemy HP pools, while Nihility debuffs help you survive the ATK multiplier. Target the "Company Time" dice face and reroll aggressively for it — it doubles all Blessing effects for 3 Domains. Bring Blade or Jingliu as main DPS since their HP-fluctuation mechanics scale hardest with Destruction Blessings.

🌀 Divergent Universe

Divergent Universe reworks the SU formula with Protocol levels (Protocol 8 is max) and Equation mechanics — pre-defined Blessing synergies that auto-activate when you collect enough matching Blessings. Equations replace the manual Resonance pick from other modes.

Protocol 8 Challenge: Enemies act twice per turn and ignore 50% of non-Critical DMG reduction. You must complete full Domains without restarting — no backtracking for missed Blessings.

Best Equations:

  • "Beneath the Old Oak" (Elation + Hunt): FUA + Crit bonanza. Best for Ratio/Feixiao teams.
  • "Blood Debt" (Destruction + Nihility): HP sacrifice + DoT amplification. Blade + Black Swan = AoE carnage.
  • "Frost Moon" (Remembrance + Erudition): Freeze chain + AoE damage. Jingliu + Herta perma-freeze comp.

🏛️ Domain Type Strategy

  • Combat → Combat: Prioritize early for 1★/2★ Blessings to build your core Path foundation. Take these in Domain 1-3.
  • Elite: Mid-run (Domain 4-7). Brings better rewards (2★/3★ Blessings) but at higher risk. Save your Resonance for these.
  • Occurrence: High-variance. Can yield Curios, Cosmic Fragments, or negative effects. Take these when you have Cosmic Fragments to spend at the Merchant.
  • Respite: Heal + upgrade Blessings. Essential mid-point stop. Always upgrade your best 3★ Blessing here.
  • Boss (Plane 3): Target these last. Your build should be 80% complete by the time you enter the final boss Domain.
  • Abundance (Event): Exclusive to specific SU versions. High reward potential but can be punishing — read the options carefully.

📌 General SU Tips

  1. Blessing Synergy > Quantity: A run with 10 carefully chosen Blessings that synergize outperforms 30 random Blessings every time. Focus on 3★ and 2★ matching your Path.
  2. Cosmic Fragments: Save 100+ Fragments for the Plane 2 Merchant (Respite Domain). You can buy 3★ Blessings or transform weak ones.
  3. Reroll Strategically: Use the reroll at the first Respite — if your Path's 3★ Blessings aren't showing, switch to a fallback Path.
  4. Path Resonance Cheat: If you're struggling with damage, pick a secondary Path that supports your main's weakness (e.g., Preservation main → Nihility secondary for debuffs).
  5. Planar Ornaments: SU is the only source for Planar Ornament sets. Use higher-difficulty runs to farm the latest sets — Protocol 8 Divergent Universe gives the best returns per run.
  6. Boss Patterns: Learn boss attack patterns. Most SU bosses have a "rage" phase below 30% HP where they hit harder — save your Resonance and Ults for this window to burst through it.
