Swarm King Guide — Simulated Universe

Quantum · Simulated Universe Boss (Swarm Disaster)
[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

The Swarm King (True Sting) is the final boss of the Swarm Disaster in Simulated Universe. It summons smaller Stings that explode on death, dealing massive AoE damage. This fight tests your blessing synergy more than your raw stats.

Mechanics

The boss spawns Lesser Stings every few turns. When a Lesser Sting dies, it explodes for AoE damage and applies debuffs. The Swarm King itself charges a devastating attack — the more Stings alive, the stronger it is.

Best Paths

  • Propagation: Thematic choice. Spore explosions handle adds
  • Preservation: Shield stacking trivializes the fight
  • Abundance: Out-heal everything with enough blessings
  • Hunt: Speed and turn manipulation — kill the boss before it kills you

Strategy

  • Don't kill all adds at once — staggered deaths prevent burst damage
  • Bring AoE DPS to manage the Sting swarm
  • Preservation path + Gepard/Fu Xuan is the safest clear
  • Focus the Swarm King, not the adds — it's a DPS race
