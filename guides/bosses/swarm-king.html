TL;DR

The Swarm King (True Sting) spawns Lesser Stings that explode on death for AoE damage. Don't kill all adds at once — stagger their deaths to avoid burst damage. Preservation path with Gepard/Fu Xuan is the safest clear.

Best Paths (Community Consensus)

PathDifficultyNotes
PreservationEasiestShields negate Sting explosions
PropagationMediumThematic, high damage but risky
AbundanceEasyOutheal everything, slower clear
HuntHardSpeed clear, high risk of oneshots

FAQ

What difficulty for first clear?

Difficulty 3. Difficulty 4+ adds mechanics that require specific team compositions. Clear D3 first to unlock Propagation path, which makes higher difficulties easier.
