Phantylia Boss Guide

Wind + Lightning · Xianzhou Luofu Final Boss
[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Phantylia the Undying is the final boss of the Xianzhou Luofu arc. She uses both Wind and Lightning attacks across two phases, and has a unique "flowers" mechanic that makes this a race against time.

Phase Mechanics

Phase 1

Phantylia summons Abundance flowers that heal her. Destroy them immediately. She alternates between single-target and AoE attacks. Debuff cleansing is important — she applies Wind Shear.

Phase 2

She transforms into a massive form. Now she summons TWO types of flowers: healing and damage-boosting. Prioritize the damage-boosting flowers first, then healing. She charges a team-wipe ultimate — you need enough DPS to kill her before she casts it twice.

Best Teams

  • Jing Yuan or any AoE DPS — flowers need to die fast
  • Buffer (Tingyun/Bronya)
  • Healer with cleanse (Luocha/Huohuo/Natasha)
  • Flex: second DPS or debuffer (Pela is great)

Strategy Tips

  • Always kill flowers on sight — they're priority #1
  • Save AoE ultimates for flower spawn waves
  • Bring a cleanser — Wind Shear stacks hurt
  • This is a DPS check in Phase 2 — don't skimp on damage
