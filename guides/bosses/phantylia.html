Phantylia Boss Guide

[Ad]

TL;DR

Phantylia summons Abundance flowers that heal her (Phase 1) and damage-boost her (Phase 2). Always kill flowers immediately — they are the top priority. Bring AoE DPS and a cleanser for Wind Shear.

Community Strategy

This is a DPS + AoE check, not a survival check. If you can kill flowers within one turn of spawning, the fight is easy. If flowers accumulate, she becomes unkillable. Jing Yuan (AoE follow-up) and Acheron (AoE ultimate) are the community's top picks for this fight.

FAQ

Is Phantylia harder than Cocolia?

Yes, about 30% more team DPS required. Cocolia is a break check. Phantylia is a pure damage check — if you can't kill flowers fast enough, you'll wipe to her enrage.
