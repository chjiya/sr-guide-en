Cocolia Boss Guide

Ice · Beloborg Final Boss
[Ad Placeholder]

Overview

Cocolia is the final boss of the Belobog arc. She's an Ice-element boss with two phases and a unique mechanic: summoning Bronya to buff her. The key is managing both targets and breaking Cocolia before her charged attack.

Phase Mechanics

Phase 1

Cocolia attacks with AoE Ice damage and summons ice pillars. Breaking them reduces her toughness. She occasionally calls Bronya for a damage buff — focus Bronya down quickly when she appears.

Phase 2

At ~50% HP, Cocolia enters an enhanced state. She charges a devastating AoE attack. You MUST break her toughness before the charge completes, or your team will take massive damage. The Trailblazer (Fire) gets a special buff in this fight — use them.

Best Teams

  • Fire Trailblazer (mandatory for story fight)
  • Any Fire/Lightning/Quantum DPS — break her quickly
  • Healer with cleanse for Freeze debuff
  • Buffer or second DPS

Strategy Tips

  • Save ultimates for Phase 2 — you need to break her fast
  • Always kill Bronya first when she spawns
  • Don't use Ice DPS — Cocolia has ice resistance
  • Fire Trailblazer's taunt can tank the big hits
