Memory of Chaos 3.4 Guide

Overview

Memory of Chaos 3.4 features enemies weak to Lightning, Fire, and Imaginary. Bring your Acheron, Firefly, or Dr. Ratio teams for the best results.

Best Teams

  • Side 1: Acheron hypercarry or Feixiao FUA — Lightning/Wind weak enemies
  • Side 2: Firefly Super Break or Jingliu hypercarry — Fire/Ice weak enemies

Strategy Tips

  • Side 1 has a boss with debuff mechanics — bring a cleanser
  • Side 2 is a pure DPS check — use your strongest team here
  • Prioritize breaking enemies before they charge their ultimates
