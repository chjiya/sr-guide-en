Tier List — Community Consensus

Aggregated from Prydwen, NGA community votes, and Reddit r/HonkaiStarRail polls. Rankings consider MoC, Pure Fiction, and Apocalyptic Shadow performance.

[Ad]

SS Tier — Best in Role

Acheron

Top 3 DPS. Universal, ignores weakness type. Needs Jiaoqiu for SS.

Firefly

Super Break core. Fastest average MoC clear. F2P-friendly build.

Feixiao

Best single-target. FUA team synergy. Excels in Apocalyptic Shadow.

Ruan Mei

Best support. Unique Break Efficiency. Fits every team. Must-pull.

S Tier — Excellent

Robin

Best FUA buffer. Universal ATK + damage procs.

Kafka

DOT enabler. Longest meta relevance (since 1.2).

Black Swan

Best DOT applier. Pairs with Kafka for S-tier DOT team.

Aventurine

Best sustain. FUA synergy + shields. Debuff on ultimate.

A Tier — Solid

Jingliu

Fallen from S. Still clears, needs more investment than SS options.

Pela

Best 4-star debuffer. Universal DEF shred.

Tingyun

Best 4-star buffer. ATK + energy battery.

Gallagher

Best 4-star sustain. Debuff on ult = Acheron synergy.

Methodology

Rankings compiled from Prydwen.gg tier list, NGA community votes, and Reddit r/HonkaiStarRail patch polls. Weighted toward MoC 12 performance (50%) with Pure Fiction (25%) and Apocalyptic Shadow (25%).
